         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  WASHINGTON  D CSenator Kennedys student loan investigation has uncovered evidence of agreements between the New Jersey Guaranty Agency and lenders that demonstrates that lenders paid the guaranty agency to market their products to schools  Kennedy  Chairman of the Health  Education  Labor and Pensions Committee  opened an investigation into the practices of the New Jersey Guaranty Agency on March 16  2007  The agency terminated the agreements with Sallie Mae and Nelnet on April 20  2007 Kennedy said  The law established Guaranty Agencies to be impartial and unbiased guardians of student interests  overseeing the conduct of private lenders in the federally guaranteed loan program  Contracts like these present a serious conflict of interest and compromise the agencies neutrality  We cannot allow guaranty agencies to become marketing departments for large for profit lenders  I applaud New Jersey Attorney General Rabners decision to investigate this important matter Documents available upon request     Laura Capps  Melissa Wagoner  202  224 2633
