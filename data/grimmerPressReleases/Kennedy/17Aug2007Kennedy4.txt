         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  WASHINGTON  DC Senator Edward M  Kennedy  Chairman of the Senate Health  Education  Labor  and Pensions Committee  and Senator Mike Enzi  Ranking Member of the HELP Committee  released the following statement today in response to the second mine accident that occurred last night in Utah  Senator Kennedy said  The loss of the rescue workers compounds this enormous tragedy for the community of Huntington and the entire country  Our hearts go out to the families and friends of all the miners and rescue workers in this time of grief  We mourn for those we have lost  and our thoughts and prayers are with the families of the trapped miners that are still waiting for some sign of hope  We owe it to all those affected by this tragedy and to mine workers everywhere to find out why this accident occurred  and how future disasters can be prevented Senator Enzi said  Diana and I join the mining community in mourning the tragic loss of three brave mine rescue professionals in Utah last night  and send our prayers and continuing support for the families of the six trapped miners and the other injured rescuers  Wyoming shares a border with Utah as well as unique mining conditions and an appreciation for the importance of placing safety above all else  We expect the Mine Safety and Health Administration to act swiftly to determine the cause of these two accidents in Utah       Laura Capps  Melissa Wagoner  202  224 2633
