         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE     Senator Edward M  Kennedy  Senator John F  Kerry  and Representative Edward J  Markey today announced a federal grant of  44 000 to the Winchester Fire Department by the Department of Homeland Security  The funds will be used by the fire department to provide emergency assistance training and to purchase new equipment    Senator Kennedy said  Winchester firefighters provide a critically important service to the community  and we should make sure they have the resources they need to do their jobs  Their courageous service deserves our strong support  and Im pleased that DHS has provided these funds to support the Department as it continues to protect the town    In a post 9 11 world  its essential that our first responders get the resources they need to help protect our state  The Winchester Fire Department should be proud it was chosen to receive this funding  its a testament to the brave men and women there  said Senator Kerry  Im grateful that DHS this made this funding possible  Its a smart investment in our future   Representative Markey said  I commend Chief John Nash and the Winchester Fire Department for putting together a strong application for this DHS grant  Winchester s brave first responders perform a vital public service and I am pleased that the federal government has met its obligation to the town by providing these important resources    Melissa Wagoner Keith Maley  617  565 4472
