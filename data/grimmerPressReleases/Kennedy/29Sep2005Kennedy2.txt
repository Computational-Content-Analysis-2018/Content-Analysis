         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE   FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE   CONTACT  Laura Capps Melissa Wagoner                 202  224 2633    I join the nation in mourning the loss of Judge Motley  She was a powerful pioneer for civil rights  and her extraordinary career has been an inspiration to the Civil Rights Movement and to the nation for more than half a century  She began her brilliant legal career with Thurgood Marshall when he was chief counsel for the NAACP s Legal Defense Fund  She helped litigate some of the early great civil rights cases  including Brown v  Board of Education  and was lead counsel for James Meredith in achieving integration at the University of Mississippi  She later became the first African American woman elected to the New York State Senate  and the first African American female federal judge  She ll be remembered forever for the enduring trails she blazed   Laura Capps  Melissa Wagoner   202  224 2633
