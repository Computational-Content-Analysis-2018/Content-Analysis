         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  WASHINGTON  D C    Senator Edward M  Kennedy released the following statement this evening upon the release of financial disclosure forms of Department of Education official  Matteo Fontana  The financial disclosure forms filed by Education Department official Matteo Fontana during his time at the Department raise grave concerns about the effectiveness and impartiality of the ethics process at the Department   The forms show that Department officials were aware that Mr  Fontana held a significant financial interest in a company that he was charged with overseeing  Any American can tell you that this is dead wrong   For the sake of college students and their families  I urge the Education Department to put an immediate stop to this troubling practice and ensure that none of its officials have any personal investments in student loan companies      Laura Capps  Melissa Wagoner  202  224 2633
