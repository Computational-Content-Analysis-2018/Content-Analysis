         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE     Senator Edward M  Kennedy announced today that North Shore Community College in Danvers  Massachusetts will receive a grant of  1 8 million under the Student Support Services program of the Department of Education     This funding is critical to North Shore s mission of seeing that every student who enrolls has the academic counseling  mentoring and other support needed to stay on course and graduate  said Senator Kennedy  Hundreds of students at North Shore  especially low income students  first generation college students  and students with disabilities  benefit from these services each year  These funds enable the college to provide the continuing support that can make all the difference in a student s achievements in college and in life  I commend North Shore for all it does for its students inside and outside the classroom    Melissa Wagoner Keith Maley  617  565 4472
