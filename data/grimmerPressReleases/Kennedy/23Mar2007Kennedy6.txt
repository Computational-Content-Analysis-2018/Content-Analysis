         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE   40 million Kennedy amendment for FDA drug safety accepted into  BudgetWASHINGTON  D C Today  Senator Edward M  Kennedy proposed an  amendment to the Budget resolution to increase by  40 million the amount of  funding the Food and Drug Administration receives for drug safety   Senator Kennedy said These needed funds will help FDA do the  job all Americans are counting on it to do    making sure that life saving  medicines are safe and effective for the patients who rely on them  I look  forward to working with my colleagues in Congress on legislation to give FDA the  tools it needs to do that essential job more effectively   Laura Capps  Melissa Wagoner  202  224 2633
