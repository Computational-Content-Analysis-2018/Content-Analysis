         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE     Washington  D C     Today  Senator Edward M  Kennedy released the following statement paying tribute to our armed forces this Memorial Day    This Memorial Day  we pay special tribute to the courageous men and women in the armed forces who gave the last full measure of devotion to our country in the war in Iraq     Men from Massachusetts are among those heroes who made the ultimate sacrifice  Whatever our feelings about the war  we honor their immense sacrifice    We have an obligation to them beyond remembrance  We owe it to all of them to care for the loved ones they can no longer protect  As we honor the memory of those heroes today  let us also renew our commitment to provideand provide generouslyfor the needs of the wives and husbands and children they loved so much
