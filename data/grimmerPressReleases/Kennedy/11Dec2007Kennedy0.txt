         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE     WASHINGTON  DC Today  Senator Edward M  Kennedy  Chairman of the Health  Education  Labor and Pensions Committee  released the following statement in response to the food safety  drug and device agreement that has been reached with China      I commend Secretary Leavitt for this achievement  These agreements are worthwhile initiatives with Chinese authorities to assure the safety and quality of food  drugs  and medical devices imported from China  and to investigate problems with Chinese products  They don t change the fact that the FDA needs new safety authority and significantly increased funds to do its job  as the FDA Science Board s recent report makes clear           Melissa Wagoner  202  224 2633
