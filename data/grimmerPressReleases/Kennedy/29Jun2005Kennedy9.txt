         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  Washington  DC  Yesterday the House approved yet another pay raise for itself yesterday    the eighth raise in the last nine years  In those same years  Congress has not raised the minimum wage once for the lowest paid worker  Senator Kennedy issued the following statement in response to the House pay raise  It s wrong of Republican leaders to abuse their power to give themselves a pay raise while rejecting a raise for America s hard working low wage workers Majority Leader DeLay claims that the pay raise is needed so members of Congress don t lose purchasing power  Yet since the last increase  minimum wage workers have lost 15  of their purchasing power while the costs of family basics like housing and health care have skyrocketed Members of Congress should not give themselves a raise until we have raised the federal minimum wage  No one who works for a living should have to live in poverty   Laura Capps Melissa Wagoner  202  224 2633
