         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE     Senator Kennedy today announced a  617 000 grant funded to Brigham and Women s Hospital to train physicians for advanced research on cardiovascular disease  The funds will be made available by the National Heart  Lung  and Blood Institute at the National Institutes of Health     Over 70 million Americans suffer from cardiovascular disease  We need to train more doctors to conduct the best possible research in this area  said Senator Kennedy  in order to develop more effective strategies for prevention and treatment  Brigham and Women s Hospital has the ability to produce excellent researchers in this field  and this investment will make it happen    Laura Capps  Melissa Wagoner   202  224 2633
