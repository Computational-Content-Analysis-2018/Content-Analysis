         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE     WASHINGTON   DC Today  Senator Edward M  Kennedy released the following statement in observance of Veterans Day  which falls on Monday  November 12th      From Lexington to Concord to Normandy to Iwo Jima to Korea to Vietnam to Iraq and Afghanistan today  our Army  Navy  Marines  Air Force and Coast Guard are part of a long line of courageous men and women who stood their ground with uncommon valor and gave so much for all of us  Today we especially remember these heroes  and we pledge to give all our veterans and their families the care they need and deserve      Veterans Day is a time for all of us to reflect on the sacrifices made by our men and women in uniform  They protect us  and we have a solemn responsibility to protect them and their families when they return home  Our veterans represent the best of America  and we owe each and every one of them an immense debt we must do our best to repay      Senator Kennedy also recorded an audio recording about Veterans Day and veterans unemployment issues  Senator Kennedy held a hearing on servicemembers unemployment issues yesterday in the United States Senate Health  Education  Labor and Pensions Committee      To access the recording you can dial  800  511 0763  the PIN number is 1127  You may also access the recording by visiting http   demradio senate gov  and selecting Kennedy          Melissa Wagoner  202  224 2633
