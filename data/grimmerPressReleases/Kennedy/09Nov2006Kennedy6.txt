         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  Washington  D C   As the country observes Veterans Day on November 11th  Senator Edward M  Kennedy released the following statement honoring our nations veterans  On this Veterans Day  America pays tribute to the service and sacrifice of all the brave men and women who have fought for our country in the past and to all those serving today in our nations armed forces here at home and around the world  These patriots put themselves in harms way to protect us all  and we owe them an immense debt of gratitude  Its because of their dedication and sacrifice and the dedication and sacrifice of their families that we continue to enjoy the freedoms enshrined in our Constitution  From the shores of Normandy  to the jungles of Vietnam  to Iraq and Afghanistan today  our soldiers  sailors  marines and air force men and women have defended America with great courage and commitment  In honoring these true American heroes on this special day we also renew our solemn commitment to care for them and their families to the best of our ability in all the years ahead    Laura Capps Melissa Wagoner  202  224 2633
