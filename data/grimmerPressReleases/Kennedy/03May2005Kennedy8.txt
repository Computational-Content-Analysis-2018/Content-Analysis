         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE     The Department of Justice Community Capacity Development Office announced today that a grant of  175 000 will be awarded to the Churches of Greater Springfield in Massachusetts for the Weed and Seed Program     The program is a partnership between law enforcement authorities and neighborhood groups to reduce gun violence and drug abuse  Its goals include identifying gaps in treatment and substance abuse services  and expanding community policing to strengthen community trust and increase cooperation with law enforcement  The affected areas include Old Hill  McNight  Bay and Upper Hill     Senator Kennedy said  We know that partnerships between Law enforcement and community groups can prevent crime and build stronger neighborhoods  Weed and Seed is an excellent example of federal dollars making practical differences in local communities  and I look forward to significant benefits from this grant for all the people of Springfield   Laura Capps  Melissa Wagoner   202  224 2633
