         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  Statement of Senator Edward M  Kennedy on the Release of the United Mineworkers  Report on Sago MineThe United Mine Workers Report clearly  demonstrates that the tragic deaths at Sago Mine were preventable  Needed mine  safety regulations have languished for too long  and Americas miners are paying  the price  The MINER Act that Congress passed into law last  year was a vital step in protecting miners safety  but more needs to be done   We need swift compliance by mining companies with the safety requirements of  the MINER Act and tough penalties for those who fail to fall into line   Laura Capps  Melissa Wagoner  202  224 2633
