         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  WASHINGTON  D CTonight  Senator Edward M  Kennedy released the following statement on the passage of the Lugar Kennedy legislation  S 1104  The bill will provide 1 000 Iraqi translators and interpreters with special immigrant visas  Kennedy  Chair of the Judiciary Committee s Subcommittee on Immigration  Border Security and Refugees held the first congressional hearing on the issue of Iraqi refugees in January of this year America has a fundamental obligation to help those brave Iraqis put their lives on the line by working for our government  This legislation granting 1 000 special immigrant visas to Iraqis who served as translators and interpreters is an important step in that direction  Its essential  however  that we work toward a far more comprehensive and effective approach to meet the needs of Iraqs countless other refugees  especially those whose service to our nation in other ways has left an assassins bulls eye on their backs       Laura Capps  Melissa Wagoner  202  224 2633
