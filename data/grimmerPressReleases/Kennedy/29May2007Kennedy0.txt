         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  WASHINGTON  D CToday  Senator Edward M  Kennedy  Chairman of the Health  Education  Labor and Pensions Committee  released the following statement in response to the decision by the Supreme Court in Ledbetter v  Goodyear Tire and Rubber Company  Todays Supreme Court decision in Ledbetter v  Goodyear Tire and Rubber Co  strikes at the heart of the civil rights laws requiring equal pay for equal work  Workers will have to challenge pay discrimination within a limited time period no matter how long the discrimination continues  Many victims of pay discrimination who didnt immediately realize they were being paid less than others will have no remedy  even though the discrimination continues with every paycheck  With women earning only 77 cents for every dollar earned by men  the nation needs strong laws against pay discrimination  This is not what Congress intended when we passed the landmark Civil Rights Act of 1991  and we need to restore full protection against wage discrimination      Laura Capps  Melissa Wagoner  202  224 2633
