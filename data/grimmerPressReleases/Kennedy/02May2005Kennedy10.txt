         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE     Senator Edward M  Kennedy announced today that the Education Development Center in Newton will receive  4 million in grants under the Math and Science Teacher Quality Research Program of the Department of Education     Today more than ever  American students need to better math and science skills in order to compete in the global economy  said Senator Kennedy  With these funds  the Education Development Center will be able to conduct critical research on how to improve math and science teaching and learning for our youngest students  I look forward to seeing the results of their research in the future     The Center will use the funds for two projects  One will develop an early science literacy program in cooperation with four Head Start programs across the state  The other project will conduct research on ways in which preschool teachers can use modern technology to improve students  language and literacy skills   Melissa Wagoner   Brent Carney  617 565 4472
