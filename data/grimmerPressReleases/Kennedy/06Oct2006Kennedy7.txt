         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE    Senator Edward M  Kennedy  Senator John F  Kerry  and Representative John F  Tierney today announced a federal grant of  129 000 to the North Reading Fire Department by the Department of Homeland Security  The funds will be used by the Fire Department to upgrade its radio communications equipment   Senator Kennedy said  Its critically important to support the Commonwealths fire fighters as they work to keep our communities safe  Im pleased that DHS has provided these funds  which will help the courageous North Reading Firefighters continue to serve and protect the community   Our brave first responders need every resource available to protect our communities  and this funding will help them do just that  said Senator Kerry  This is a smart investment and the North Reading Fire Department should be proud it was chosen to receive these resources  I commend DHS for making this smart investment in Massachusetts   It is critical that we provide firefighters and other first responders with the resources they need to protect our community  I am very pleased North Reading firefighters will have the money they need to make upgrades to their radio communications equipment which will help enhance the Departments ability to respond to emergency situations  said Congressman John F  Tierney    Melissa Wagoner Keith Maley  617  565 4472
