         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  Statement of Edward M  Kennedy on Bush Administration Agreeing to Issue  Regulation on Protective EquipmentThe Bush Administration  under  pressure from lawsuits filed in federal court  agreed to issue a long overdue  final rule requiring employers to pay for essential safety equipment for their  employees  The rule had been revised by the Occupational Safety and Health  Administration near the end of the Clinton Administration  but the Bush  Administration had refused to make it final until now Senator Edward M   Kennedy said  Twenty million American workers have had to wait seven years for  the protection they deserve from serious dangers on the job  Theyd still be  waiting if we didnt have a Democratic Congress  and well be vigilant to make  sure the final rule is strong enough   Laura Capps  Melissa Wagoner  202  224 2633
