         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE   FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE    Washington  D C     Today  Senator Edward M  Kennedy released the following statement on the inauguration of President Preval of Haiti    The inauguration today of President Rene Preval of Haiti demonstrated to the world the determination of the Haitian people to bring change and reform to their government  I commend the people of Haiti and President Preval  and I look forward to his follow through on his campaign pledges to eliminate corruption  strengthen basic institutions  establish security  reform the police and judiciary  and encourage private investment and economic development  These goals are all essential to Haiti s future  and Congress and the Bush Administration should support them with more than rhetoric  I look forward to this hopeful new era for all the people of Haiti   Laura Capps Melissa Wagoner  202  224 2633
