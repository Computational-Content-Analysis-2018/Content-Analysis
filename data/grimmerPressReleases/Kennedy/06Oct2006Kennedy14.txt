         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE   Senator Edward M  Kennedy  Senator John F  Kerry  and Representative John W  Olver today announced a federal grant of  64 000 to the Russell Fire Department by the Department of Homeland Security  The funds will be used by the Fire Department to make sure its protective gear meet the highest standards of safety   Senator Kennedy said  The Russell Fire Department courageously serves its community every day  and its critically important for it to have the resources they need  I commend DHS for providing these funds  and I thank the brave firefighters of Russell for all they do so well   This funding will provide the Russell Fire Department with the resources it needs to keep Massachusetts communities safe  said Senator Kerry  This is a smart investment in our first responders and I commend DHS for making it possible   Our first responders deserve the most up to date equipment to be able to do their jobs safely and effectively  said Congressman John Olver  D MA   This funding will go a long way in helping to keep our firefighters and all those they protect  safe    Melissa Wagoner Keith Maley  617  565 4472
