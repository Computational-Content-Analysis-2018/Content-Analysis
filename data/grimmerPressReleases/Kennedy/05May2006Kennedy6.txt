         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE     Washington  D C     Almost 9 months after the devastation of Hurricanes Katrina and Rita  families displaced by the storms are still struggling to find work  The unemployment rate among Katrina evacuees who have not returned home is a shocking 26 5 percent  More than 75 000 evacuees will lose their unemployment benefits on June 3  Senator Kennedy issued the following statement calling for prompt action to give storm victims the help they deserve    Today s employment report reveals a major problem Congress can t keep ignoring  An astounding number of those displaced by the Gulf Coast storms are still struggling to find jobs  These hardworking men and women continue to face huge obstacles  Unless we act this month  their current unemployment benefits will expire  Congress should act immediately to extend these benefits at least through the end of the year in order to prevent an unjustified new hardship for these struggling hurricane victims and their families    Laura Capps Melissa Wagoner  202  224 2633
