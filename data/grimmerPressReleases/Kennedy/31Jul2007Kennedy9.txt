         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  WASHINGTON  DC Today  Senator Edward M  Kennedy  Chairman of the Senate Health  Education  Labor and Pensions Committee  released the following statement in response to the passage by the United States House of Representatives of the Ledbetter Fair Pay Act of 2007  No American should be denied equal pay for equal work  Employees ability to provide for their children  save for retirement  and enjoy the benefit of their labor should not be limited by discrimination  The Courts decision undermined these bedrock principles by imposing unrealistically short time limits on such claims  Im pleased to cosponsor the bill with a bipartisan group of colleagues in the Senate  and I look forward to moving the bill forward to finally correct this basic injustice      Laura Capps  Melissa Wagoner  202  224 2633
