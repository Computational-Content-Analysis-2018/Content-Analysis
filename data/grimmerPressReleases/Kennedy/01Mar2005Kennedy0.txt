         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  Senator Edward M  Kennedy and John F  Kerry and Representative John Tierney today announced  180 790 in funding for the Peabody Fire Department  These funds are part of the Department of Homeland Security s Office for Preparedness  Assistance to Firefighters Grants program  Firefighters are on the front lines of protecting our communities  They risk their lives to keep our families safe and our homeland secure  This funding will go a long way in helping these brave men and women continue the extraordinary job they do for all those in their communities  Senator Kennedy said of the funding   Firefighters are on our frontlines of defense every day  whether its homeland security or protecting our families in an emergency  We need to be sure they have the tools they need  This grant for the Peabody Fire Department is important to keeping these heroes and our communities safe  said Senator Kerry  This federal grant will assist in purchasing the operations and safety equipment that the Peabody Fire Department needs to stay as safe as possible while doing their dangerous jobs  Despite the President s neglect of first responders in his budget proposal  I will continue to work with cities and towns throughout the 6th district to garner as much federal support for our firehouses  police stations  and emergency response teams as possible   These funds have been awarded to the Peabody Fire Department as part of the Operations and Firefighter Safety Program  Funding through this program can be used for training  equipment  personal protective equipment  wellness and fitness  and modifications to fire stations and facilities
