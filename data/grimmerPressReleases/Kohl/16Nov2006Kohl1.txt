KOHL TO JOIN SENATE BANKING COMMITTEE IN  07                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    November 16  2006    Phone   202  224 5653        KOHL TO JOIN SENATE BANKING COMMITTEE IN  07     Washington  DC    U S  Senator Herb Kohl has been named to serve on the Senate Banking  Housing and Urban Affairs Committee when the 110th Congress convenes next year  The Banking Committee has jurisdiction over a variety of banking and securities related issues as well as housing  international trade  and Federal monetary policy  Kohl   I ve been in business all my life  I m looking forward to working with Senator Dodd and our colleagues on the Banking Committee on issues that impact businesses big and small in Wisconsin and across our country   In the new Congress  Kohl is also expected to serve as Chairman of three committees    Appropriations Subcommittee on Agriculture  Rural Development and Related Agencies  Judiciary Subcommittee on Antitrust  Competition Policy and Consumer Rights   Special Committee on Aging  The Banking Committee will be led by Senator Christopher Dodd  a 5 term Democrat from Connecticut  Banking subcommittee assignments will be determined next year  All committee appointments for 2007 are subject to Democratic steering committee and conference approval
