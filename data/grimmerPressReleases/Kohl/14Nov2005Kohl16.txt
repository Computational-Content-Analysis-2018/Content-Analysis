KOHL SECURES  192 000 FOR PORT WASHINGTON HARBOR PROJECT                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    November 14  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        KOHL SECURES  192 000 FOR PORT WASHINGTON HARBOR PROJECT      WASHINGTON  D C   U S  Senator Herb Kohl announced today that he secured  192 000 in federal funding for maintenance of the north breakwater at Port Washington Harbor as part of the FY 2006 Energy and Water Appropriations conference report which passed the Senate today  Sen  Kohl is a member of Senate Appropriations Committee and had identified this project as a priority   The Port Washington Harbor is essential not only for commercial purposes  but for recreation as well   said Kohl   Because of this funding  necessary maintenance and preservation of the harbor breakwater will be possible   The project provides an outer stilling basin enclosed by a converging north breakwater and shore connection   The north breakwater is in need of rehabilitation and maintenance because it has deteriorated and has chunks of concrete and fill stone missing   The breakwater has deteriorated due to wave action and ice conditions
