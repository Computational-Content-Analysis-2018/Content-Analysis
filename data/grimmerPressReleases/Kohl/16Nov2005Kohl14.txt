KOHL ANNOUNCES FUNDS FOR ST CROIX METH PROSECUTION PROJECT                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    November 16  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        KOHL ANNOUNCES FUNDS FOR ST CROIX METH PROSECUTION PROJECT     WASHINGTON  D C     Senator Herb Kohl  D WI  today announced he has secured  55 000 dollars as part of the Commerce  Justice and Science  CJS  Appropriation conference report for the St  Croix County District Attorney s Office of Meth  Prosecution Project  These funds will allow the St  Croix District Attorney to continue funding a full time staff member to guarantee timely and effective processing of methamphetamine cases  track methamphetamine related statistics and make referrals to appropriate drug treatment   Sen  Kohl is a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee and had identified this project as a priority for funding  The Senate today passed the CJS conference report today by a vote of 96 5  Meth is a terrible problem that affects not just the user but the whole community   Kohl said   Prosecuting those that use and produce Meth is an important step in curtailing its devastating effect on so many victims   Judicial officials in St  Croix County report a significant increase in the number of methamphetamine related arrests  Stemming the detrimental effect of meth on the community depends on quick tracking and processing of these arrests   The appropriation secured by Kohl will aid the District Attorney s office in processing and prosecuting their high number of methamphetamine cases
