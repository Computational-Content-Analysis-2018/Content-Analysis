Statement of U S  Senator Herb Kohl on Former Governor and U S  Senator Gaylord Nelson                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    July 3  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        Statement of U S  Senator Herb Kohl on Former Governor and U S  Senator Gaylord Nelson       Gaylord Nelson was an outstanding man and a devoted public servant   He leaves a tremendous legacy of protecting the environment  but his work touched many other lives too   He was well liked and respected  even revered  during the span of his career   I will miss him and I wish his family peace
