SENATOR KOHL STATEMENT ON THE PASSING OF ROSA PARKS                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    October 25  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        SENATOR KOHL STATEMENT ON THE PASSING OF ROSA PARKS     WASHINGTON  D C     The bravery that Rosa Parks exhibited on that winter day in 1955 will forever be remembered as the fire behind the civil rights movement    a movement that still continues today  Ms  Parks will be greatly missed by all that knew her as a person  but the legacy she leaves in her wake is one that still positively impacts millions of lives today
