STATEMENT OF U S  SENATOR KOHL ON THE ADMINISTRATION S FY2006 BUDGET PROPOSAL                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    Febrary 7  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        STATEMENT OF U S  SENATOR KOHL ON THE ADMINISTRATION S FY2006 BUDGET PROPOSAL     This budget we received today is only the first step in a long process of setting the government s priorities for the fiscal year  As this process moves forward  we should be committed to lowering the deficit  but we should not do so at the expense of meeting other pressing needs  including creating new jobs  ensuring that our troops have the resources to complete their mission  protecting our homeland  and funding education programs
