KOHL SECURES  500 000 TO IMPROVE LAW ENFORCEMENT COMMUNICATION IN WISCONSIN                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    November 16  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        KOHL SECURES  500 000 TO IMPROVE LAW ENFORCEMENT COMMUNICATION IN WISCONSIN     WASHINGTON  D C     Senator Herb Kohl  D WI  today announced that he has secured  500 000 the Justice Gateway  a one stop  easy access database for local  State and federal criminal justice agencies in Wisconsin  These funds were included in the  Commerce  Justice and Science  CJS  Appropriation conference report which passed the Senate by a vote of 96 5     Law enforcement agencies lose too much time and energy dealing with mountains of paperwork   said Kohl   By utilizing better technology  our justice system can more efficiently and effectively solve crimes and put offenders behind bars    The Justice Gateway will eliminate the labor intensive paper process of reporting crime statistics   Agencies will electronically notify each other with critical information over a secure  permission based central directory    The Justice Gateway project was requested by the Wisconsin Chiefs of Police Association along with the Governor s Office of Justice Assistance  When complete  the project will bridge gaps in communication by linking 524 local law enforcement agencies  72 County Sheriffs  the Wisconsin State Patrol  state agencies including the Office of Justice Assistance and Departments of Transportation  Administration  and Corrections  and the Wisconsin Public Defenders Office   It will also connect the Wisconsin judicial system including the Wisconsin Supreme Court  four appellate districts  69 circuit courts and 71 District Attorneys
