KOHL SECURES  10 6 MILLION FOR FOREST PRODUCTS LAB PROJECTS                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    June 29  2006    Phone   202  224 5653        KOHL SECURES  10 6 MILLION FOR FOREST PRODUCTS LAB PROJECTS     WASHINGTON  D C     U S  Senator Herb Kohl announced today that he has secured  9 144 million in federal funding for facilities improvements at the Forest Products Laboratory  FPL  in Madison and  1 5 million for their Advanced Wood Structure Research program  The funding was included as part of the FY 2007 Interior Appropriations bill  which the Senate Appropriations Committee approved this afternoon   Kohl is a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee and identified these projects as priorities for funding     We re fortunate that Wisconsin is home to so many of the country s most respected research facilities  and Forest Products Lab is considered the best in its field   Kohl said    I am proud to direct funding to an institution that is using cutting edge techniques to study recycling and develop environmentally friendly technology   The FPL in Madison is the nation s leading wood research institute  Their research has led to innovations in recycling and ecological technology that has resulted in a greater yield from existing lumber and reduced demand on forest resources   The scientists at FPL work in conjunction with universities  industries  and federal and state agencies   The Interior Appropriations bill must now go before the full Senate for consideration
