KOHL SECURES  7 MILLION FOR MILWAUKEE AIR NATIONAL GUARD                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    September 22  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        KOHL SECURES  7 MILLION FOR MILWAUKEE AIR NATIONAL GUARD     WASHINGTON  D C  U S  Senator Herb Kohl announced today that he secured  7 million in federal funding to expand the current maintenance facilities of the 128th Air Refueling Wing at General Mitchell International Airport in Milwaukee   This funding was included in the fiscal year 2006 Military Construction Appropriations bill  which the Senate approved today  Kohl is a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee and had identified the Air National Guard maintenance expansion as a priority for funding   The maintenance crews of the 128th do an outstanding job in less than perfect conditions  Their talent and professionalism makes us all proud of their efforts  Federal support is crucial to keeping our facilities in top working order and I m pleased that we re able to contribute to this expansion   Kohl said  This project would expand the current maintenance facilities of the 128th Air Refueling Wing   Currently maintenance functions are spread between a variety of buildings  and none of those functions has sufficient space   Most maintenance functions operate with 40  60  less space than they need   This construction would bring all the different maintenance areas under one roof and provide adequate space   These improvements will increase efficiency since maintenance will not have to operate across ten different buildings   The project also complies with the long term base master plan and meets new Air Force criteria   The Military Construction Appropriations bill must now be approved by a Senate House conference committee before it is signed by the President
