Statement of U S  Senator Herb Kohl on the President s Address to the Nation on Iraq                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    January 10  2007    Phone   202  224 5653        Statement of U S  Senator Herb Kohl on the President s Address to the Nation on Iraq      I heard little encouraging in the President s remarks tonight   Against the advice of many of his senior commanders  the Iraq Study Group  and a clear majority of the American people  he is proposing to send more troops to Iraq    an approach we have tried before that has failed to reduce violence in the region   The Iraqis need to take responsibility for their own security   Our soldiers have been stretched to the breaking point  and we can t afford an escalation of our commitment in Iraq
