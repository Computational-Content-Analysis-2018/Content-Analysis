Statement of Senator Kohl on President s Veto of the Children s Health Insurance Bill                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    October 3  2007    Phone   202  224 5653        Statement of Senator Kohl on President s Veto of the Children s Health Insurance Bill      I am deeply disappointed by the President s decision today to veto a bipartisan and responsible measure to renew the Children s Health Insurance Program  CHIP   He s deserting the more than 100 000 Wisconsin children and their families that rely on this efficient and effective program for their health care needs  In the coming weeks  I hope our entire Wisconsin Congressional delegation will work together in a bipartisan way and vote to override the President s veto
