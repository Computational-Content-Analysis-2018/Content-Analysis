KOHL SECURES  300 000 FOR AFFORDABLE HOUSING IN APPLETON                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    November 18  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        KOHL SECURES  300 000 FOR AFFORDABLE HOUSING IN APPLETON      WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Herb Kohl announced today that the Senate appropriations bill that funds the Department of Housing and Urban Development includes  300 000 for the construction of affordable housing in Appleton  The Senate passed the Treasury  Transportation  Housing and Urban Development Appropriations bill conference report late this afternoon   Kohl is a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee and had identified this project as a priority for funding     Everyone has a right to affordable housing   said Kohl   This funding will mean that many more Appleton residents will find a place to call home  as well as spur important economic growth and development     The federal funding would be used for the construction of affordable housing at the site of the old Appleton Wire Works Factory  The Appleton Wire Works building is an historic property  which will be preserved by the development of housing at the site  Construction will include  demolition of portions of the building that were added at a time outside the period of historic significance  abatement of asbestos and lead paint  reconstruction of remaining buildings for unit development  re roofing and installation of new mechanicals  plumbing  heating  electrical and fire protections    The Senate Treasury  Transportation  Housing and Urban Development appropriations bill now goes to the President to be signed into law
