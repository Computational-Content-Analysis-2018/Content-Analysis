Statement of U S  Senator Kohl on Pope John Paul II                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    April 2  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        Statement of U S  Senator Kohl on Pope John Paul II     Pope John Paul II was a man of great compassion  spirituality  and intellect and he will be deeply missed  He left his remarkable imprint on the generations he touched and the world he helped shape  He will be remembered by many for the legacy of peace and freedom he worked so tirelessly to advance
