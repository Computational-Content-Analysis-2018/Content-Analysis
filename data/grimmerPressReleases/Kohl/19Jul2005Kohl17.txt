KOHL SECURES  300 000 FOR AFFORDABLE HOUSING IN APPLETON                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    July 19  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        KOHL SECURES  300 000 FOR AFFORDABLE HOUSING IN APPLETON     WASHINGTON  D C  B U S  Senator Herb Kohl announced today that the Senate appropriations bill that funds the Department of Housing and Urban Development includes  300 000 for the construction of affordable housing in Appleton  Kohl is a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee and had identified this project as a priority for funding    Affordable housing is important not just for those seeking housing but for the community at large   said Sen  Kohl   When we are able to increase affordable housing we are able to help promote economic growth and development    The federal funding would be used for the construction of affordable housing at the site of the old Appleton Wire Works Factory  The Appleton Wire Works building is an historic property  which will be preserved by the development of housing at the site  Construction will include  demolition of portions of the building that were added at a time outside the period of historic significance  abatement of asbestos and lead paint  reconstruction of remaining buildings for unit development  re roofing and installation of new mechanicals  plumbing  heating  electrical and fire protections   The FY2006 funding bill for the Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD  was passed by the Senate Subcommittee on Transportation  Treasury  the Judiciary and HUD today and  is expected to be approved by the full Appropriations Committee on Thursday
