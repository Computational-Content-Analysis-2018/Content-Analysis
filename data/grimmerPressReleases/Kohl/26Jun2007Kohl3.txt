KOHL LAUDS SELECTION OF UW MADISON FOR NEW BIOENERGY RESEARCH CENTER                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    June 26  2007    Phone   202  224 5653        KOHL LAUDS SELECTION OF UW MADISON FOR NEW BIOENERGY RESEARCH CENTER     WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Herb Kohl today congratulated the University of Wisconsin    Madison and Michigan State University for their selection by the U S  Department of Energy  DOE  to collaboratively operate one of three new Bioenergy Research Centers  Earlier this year  Kohl led a Wisconsin and Michigan delegation effort to promote the joint proposal   For many years now  UW Madison has led the way in developing new technologies that make their way seamlessly from the research lab to our farms  Today s announcement is a reflection of that  but more importantly  it solidifies our state and our flagship University s position at the head of the burgeoning renewable fuels industry  As we wean our way off oil  gas  and coal energy sources  we know that more and more Americans will turn to biofuels to heat their homes and fuel their cars    biofuels developed and produced in Wisconsin   Kohl said            According to DOE Secretary Samuel Bodman  these innovative centers will accelerate basic research in the development of cellulosic ethanol and other biofuels  and make biofuel production cost competitive on a national scale by 2012 furthering our national energy security
