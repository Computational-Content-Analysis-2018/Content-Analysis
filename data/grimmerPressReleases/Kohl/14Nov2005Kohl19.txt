KOHL SECURES  2 MILLION FOR MILWAUKEE S LAKESHORE STATE PARK                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    November 14  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        KOHL SECURES  2 MILLION FOR MILWAUKEE S LAKESHORE STATE PARK     WASHINGTON  D C  B U S  Senator Herb Kohl announced today that he secured  2 million in federal funding for Lakeshore State Park in the Milwaukee Harbor as part of the FY 2006 Energy and Water Appropriations conference report which passed the Senate today   Lakeshore State Park is enjoyed by all a center for recreation and entertainment   said Kohl   adding to the quality of life for Milwaukeeans and this money will help move the pending construction projects forward   Kohl said  The project involves the construction of breakwaters and revetments to create a maritime basin and harbor of refuge   The funding will allow the Corps to continue planning and construction of the Lakeshore State Park breakwater project   Bidding for the construction contract is scheduled for August of 2005 and construction is expected in spring and summer of 2006
