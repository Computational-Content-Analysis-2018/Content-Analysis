KOHL SECURES  259 000 FOR KEWAUNEE HARBOR PROTECTION                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    November 14  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        KOHL SECURES  259 000 FOR KEWAUNEE HARBOR PROTECTION      WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Herb Kohl announced today that he secured  259 000 in federal funding for Kewaunee Harbor as part of the FY 2006 Energy and Water Appropriations conference report which was passed today by the Senate     Kewaunee Harbor serves an important purpose  both historically and economically   said Kohl   And this project will help in ensuring that it can be enjoyed by future generations   said Kohl   The project provides for an outer basin protected by a converging north breakwater and shore connection  south and north piers  an entrance channel  a northerly channel extension and two turning basins   The structures at the project need maintenance due to deterioration from wave action and ice conditions
