KOHL LAUDS  3 9M GRANT TO ADDRESS LEAD HAZARDS IN MILWAUKEE                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    January 5  2007    Phone   202  224 5653        KOHL LAUDS  3 9M GRANT TO ADDRESS LEAD HAZARDS IN MILWAUKEE     Washington  DC    U S  Senator Herb Kohl  D WI  today congratulated the City of Milwaukee Health Department on winning a  3 9 million federal grant to boost their Lead Hazard Reduction Demonstration Program  a proactive campaign to abate lead hazards in area homes and buildings     I am very pleased that Milwaukee will continue to receive this critically needed funding to clean up lead in our city s older homes   Kohl said   Children are especially vulnerable to lead exposure so the sooner we are able to remove lead from their homes  the better   The grant  awarded by the Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD   will be used to remove lead paint and building materials from an estimated 1 200 homes across the city    Milwaukee s lead abatement and education program is considered a national model and the city is one of only a few on track to be lead free by 2010    Last year  Kohl sent a letter of support to HUD Secretary Alfonso Jackson  urging the Secretary to approve Milwaukee s grant request
