KOHL ANNOUNCES  500 000 FOR MADISON POLICE INFORMATION SHARING PROJECT                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    November 16  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        KOHL ANNOUNCES  500 000 FOR MADISON POLICE INFORMATION SHARING PROJECT      Washington  DC U S  Senator Herb Kohl announced that he has secured  500 000 for the City of Madison Police Department Interoperability Project as part of the Commerce  Justice and Science  CJS  Appropriation conference report which passed the Senate by a vote of 96 5   These funds will allow the City of Madison Police Department to collect crime related data and share it with other Dane County law enforcement agencies  Sen  Kohl is a member of the Senate Appropriations committee and had identified this project as a priority for funding    As 9 11 demonstrated so dramatically  our first responders the best technology to be able to do their job effectively   said Sen  Kohl   With these funds  the City of Madison Police Department will be able to build communication and information sharing systems that will help them better protect citizens throughout Dane County   The Madison Police Department is in the process of developing an upgraded information sharing system  When complete  the Department will be able to access all law enforcement files and statistics in towns  villages and cities throughout Dane County
