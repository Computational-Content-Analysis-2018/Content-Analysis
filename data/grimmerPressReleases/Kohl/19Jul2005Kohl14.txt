KOHL SECURES  200 000 FOR THE CENTER FOR MANUFACTURING EXCELLENCE IN LaCROSSE                      Press Release      For Immediate Release    July 19  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        KOHL SECURES  200 000 FOR THE CENTER FOR MANUFACTURING EXCELLENCE IN LaCROSSE         WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Herb Kohl announced today that the Senate appropriations bill that funds the Department of Housing and Urban Development includes  200 000 for an economic development initiative in LaCrosse  Included in the fiscal year 2006 appropriations bill is  200 000 for the construction of a Center for Manufacturing Excellence in LaCrosse  Kohl is a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee and had identified this project as a priority for funding  Every year the appropriations process gives us the opportunity to invest federal dollars in communities throughout Wisconsin   Kohl said    This promotes economic development while giving our residents a return on their tax dollars  This is a worthy project whose benefits will outweigh its costs and I am pleased the Senate approved this funding    The Center for Manufacturing Excellence will be a regionally focused and quick response manufacturing training and entrepreneur center  This Center will serve as a place for companies looking to use collaborative efforts to conduct training and or develop new product lines and bring such products to market  The federal funds will be used as part of the total funds needed to construct the Center for Manufacturing Excellence in the International Business Park  The FY2006 funding bill for the Department of Housing and Urban Development  HUD  was passed by the Senate Subcommittee on Transportation  Treasury  the Judiciary and HUD today and  is expected to be approved by the full Appropriations Committee on Thursday
