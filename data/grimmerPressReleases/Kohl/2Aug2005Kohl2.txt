Statement of U S  Senator Herb Kohl on President Bush s Recess Appointment of John Bolton as U N  Ambassador                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    August 2  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        Statement of U S  Senator Herb Kohl on President Bush s Recess Appointment of John Bolton as U N  Ambassador     I m disappointed that the President s decision to appoint John Bolton as Ambassador to the United Nations shows a disregard for the many legitimate concerns that have been raised about his record  his management style and his attitudes toward the U N   For those reasons I opposed his nomination  and I continue to have serious misgivings about the message we send to the rest of the world through this appointment
