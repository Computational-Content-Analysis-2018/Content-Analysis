KOHL ANNOUNCES   300 000 FOR AFFORDABLE HOUSING IN APPLETON                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    October 21  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        KOHL ANNOUNCES   300 000 FOR AFFORDABLE HOUSING IN APPLETON      WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Herb Kohl announced today that the Senate appropriations bill that funds the Department of Housing and Urban Development includes  300 000 for the construction of affordable housing in Appleton  Late last evening the Senate passed the Treasury  Transportation  Housing and Urban Development Appropriations bill by a vote of 93 to 1  Kohl is a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee and had identified this project as a priority for funding     Affordable housing programs like this one in Appleton benefit the whole community   said Sen  Kohl   They promote economic growth and reinforce the area s sense of community    The federal funding would be used for the construction of affordable housing at the site of the historic Appleton Wire Works Factory property   The project will include  demolition of portions of the building that were added at a time outside the period of historic significance  abatement of asbestos and lead paint  reconstruction of remaining buildings for unit development  re roofing and installation of new mechanicals  plumbing  heating  electrical and fire protections   The Senate Treasury  Transportation  Housing and Urban Development appropriations bill now goes to a joint Senate House conference committee for approval before it can be signed into law by the President
