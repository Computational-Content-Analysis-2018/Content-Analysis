KOHL SECURES  231 000 FOR THE STURGEON BAY HARBOR AND LAKE MICHIGAN SHIP CANAL                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    November 14  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        KOHL SECURES  231 000 FOR THE STURGEON BAY HARBOR AND LAKE MICHIGAN SHIP CANAL     WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Herb Kohl announced today that he secured  231 000 in federal funding for rehabilitation of Section N of the Sturgeon Bay Harbor and Lake Michigan Ship Canal as part of the FY 2006 Energy and Water Appropriations conference report which passed the Senate today  Sen  Kohl is a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee and had identified this project as a priority   This project will help rehabilitate a major economic engine for Sturgeon Bay area   said Kohl   Rehabilitating this canal to ensure its efficiency and safety is necessary to keeping the Sturgeon Bay Harbor and Lake Michigan Ship Canal both efficient and safe   Section N of the south revetment is in need of rehabilitation due to the deterioration of the existing timber piling structure  The overall condition of the sheeting wall is in poor condition and the tie rods have loosened   Sediment is moving through the structure and causing a hardship for vessels traveling through the canal   Failure of the timber sheeting or tie rods would jeopardize the navigable channel
