Statement of U S  Senator Herb Kohl on the Confirmation of Robert Gates as Defense Secretary Today                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    December 6  2006    Phone   202  224 5653        Statement of U S  Senator Herb Kohl on the Confirmation of Robert Gates as Defense Secretary Today     Washington  DC    US Senator Herb Kohl  D WI  released the following statement today on the confirmation of Robert Gates to the position of Secretary of Defense  Kohl voted in favor of the Gates nomination    Robert Gates has made it clear that he has learned from past mistakes  and is prepared for the difficult challenge of Iraq   His candor before the Armed Services Committee yesterday was refreshing  and his appointment should help to bring about change in our policy in Iraq  and the Pentagon s relationship with Congress
