Statement of U S  Senator Herb Kohl on the Resignation of Secretary Rumsfeld                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    November 8  2006    Phone   202  224 5653        Statement of U S  Senator Herb Kohl on the Resignation of Secretary Rumsfeld     The American people sent a strong message to the President that they want him to radically change his conduct of the Iraq war  and I agree with that   Secretary Rumsfeld s resignation hopefully indicates that the President understands and accepts that message
