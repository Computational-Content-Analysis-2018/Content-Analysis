Statement of U S  Senator Herb Kohl on the Base Realignment   Closure Commission Decision on Fort McCoy                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    August 24  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        Statement of U S  Senator Herb Kohl on the Base Realignment   Closure Commission Decision on Fort McCoy      I m pleased that the Commission recognized the value of Fort McCoy as an excellent military training facility   The people at Fort McCoy worked aggressively to ensure that their facility is unsurpassed when it comes to combat preparation and battle simulation   Fort McCoy will continue to have a strong imprint on our military and we look forward to continuing our work with them as the new regional readiness command relocates
