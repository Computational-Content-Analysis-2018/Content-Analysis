STATEMENT OF U S  SENATOR HERB KOHL ON FEMA s DECISION TO DENY DISASTER AID FOR WISCONSIN COMMUNITIES HIT BY TORNADOES                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    September 23  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        STATEMENT OF U S  SENATOR HERB KOHL ON FEMA s DECISION TO DENY DISASTER AID FOR WISCONSIN COMMUNITIES HIT BY TORNADOES     I am disappointed by FEMA s decision to deny disaster assistance to those Wisconsin communities who are truly in need as a result of the August 18th tornadoes  These tornadoes had a devastating impact on the lives and businesses of the area  I support Gov  Doyle s appeal and will continue to work with the Wisconsin delegation to make sure the Federal government plays a role in helping the affected communities recover
