Senator Kohl s Statement on Midwest Airlines rejection of AirTran s takeover bid                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    April 13  2007     Phone   202  224 5653        Senator Kohl s Statement on Midwest Airlines rejection of AirTran s takeover bid     In rejecting AirTran s bid today  Midwest Airlines has stayed true to form  never compromising quality and service just to pad their bottom line  Midwest is a unique company in the airline industry    it is actually beloved by its customers  No one doubts that the quality of Midwest s service will suffer should AirTran complete this acquisition  Count me as one of the thousands of frequent flyers that are pleased by today s vote
