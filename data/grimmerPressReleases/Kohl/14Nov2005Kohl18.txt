KOHL SECURES  149 000 FOR ASHLAND HARBOR BREAKWATER                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    November 14  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        KOHL SECURES  149 000 FOR ASHLAND HARBOR BREAKWATER     WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Herb Kohl announced today that he secured  149 000 in federal funding for rehabilitation of the breakwater at Ashland Harbor  The funding is included in the FY 2006 Energy and Water Appropriations conference report which passed the Senate today  Kohl is a member of the Committee and had identified this project as a priority for funding     The importance of maintaining our aquatic infrastructure can not be overstated   said Sen  Kohl   This money will ensure that Ashland remains a productive and safe harbor for ships  as well as a recreation site for the people of Ashland    The harbor is protected by an 8 000 foot breakwater that is in fair condition  Breakwater deterioration has occurred due to wave action and ice conditions   This breakwater is vital for providing protection from waves and preventing damage to the shoreline and ships in the harbor
