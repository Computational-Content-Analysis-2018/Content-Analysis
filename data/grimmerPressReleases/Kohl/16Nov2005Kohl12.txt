KOHL SECURES  250 000 FOR MILWAUKEE YOUTH SUMMER STAR S                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    November 16  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        KOHL SECURES  250 000 FOR MILWAUKEE YOUTH SUMMER STAR S     WASHINGTON  D C     Senator Herb Kohl  D WI  today announced he has secured  250 000 dollars as part of the Commerce  Justice and Science Appropriation bill for the Summer Star s Program  a collaboration between Milwaukee Public Schools and local law enforcement to provide an eight week recreational and educational session for at risk teens ages 13 to 17   These funds were included in the Commerce  Justice and Science  CJS  Appropriation conference report which passed the Senate by a vote of 96 5     Summer Stars is a total success   said Kohl   The kids love it  and the results are amazing    I hope this money helps involve even more kids in this wonderful program   While many teen programs struggle to retain attendance  the number of young people participating in Summer Stars has increased annually  The program works to reduce the area juvenile crime rate by promoting positive life choices and social development with counseling  guidance with drug alcohol issues and vocational planning   Program organizers credit Summer Stars as a contributing factor in the annual drop in youth related offenses reported by the Milwaukee Police Department
