Statement of U S  Senator Herb Kohl on Chief Justice William Rehnquist                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    September 4  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        Statement of U S  Senator Herb Kohl on Chief Justice William Rehnquist     We are saddened by the loss of Chief Justice Rehnquist and our thoughts and prayers are with his family today   He carried out the awesome responsibility of leading the judicial branch of our government with honor  dignity  and forthrightness   In more than thirty years of service on the Court  he helped establish the legal framework that supports what and who we are as a nation   Chief Justice Rehnquist even handedly led the court through turbulent times  presiding over major decisions that have defined the legal contours of every aspect of American life   For that  he has earned the gratitude of the nation
