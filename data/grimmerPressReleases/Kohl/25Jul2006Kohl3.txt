Kohl Introduces Legislation to Restore Fox Valley Physician Positions                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    July 25  2006    Phone   202  224 5653        Kohl Introduces Legislation to Restore Fox Valley Physician Positions     Washington  DC    U S  Senator Herb Kohl  D WI  today introduced legislation to restore immediately Medicare funding for resident doctor positions at Fox Valley Family Medicine in Appleton   Families in Appleton shouldn t be punished by a paperwork mix up in Washington   Kohl said   Medicare wrongly cut off funding for these resident doctor positions at Fox Valley  and they need to correct their mistake   Last year  a flawed Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services  CMS  audit reduced funding for Fox Valley s Residency Program  Although Fox Valley won their subsequent appeal  a CMS regulation has held up the reinstatement of funding  Kohl s bill  the Medicare Residency Program Fairness Act of 2006  would change this regulation and order CMS to restore funding to hospitals that successfully appeal audits
