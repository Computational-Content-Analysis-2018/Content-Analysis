Statement of U S  Senator Herb Kohl on the Bombings in London                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    July 7  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        Statement of U S  Senator Herb Kohl on the Bombings in London     We share in the grief of the people of Great Britain  our steadfast ally in the war against terror   This is a stark reminder that we face a ruthless and relentless enemy who commits these atrocities because they fear the spread of freedom   I have no doubt that we will work closely with British law enforcement to bring to justice those behind today s bombings  and that our own government will remain committed to preventing attacks on our homeland
