KOHL NAMED TO DEFENSE APPROPRIATIONS PANEL                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    January 10  2007    Phone   202  224 5653        KOHL NAMED TO DEFENSE APPROPRIATIONS PANEL     WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Herb Kohl has been named to serve on the Senate Appropriations Subcommittee on Defense   The Defense Appropriations Subcommittee has jurisdiction over the Pentagon s annual budget   Kohl has been member of the Appropriations Committee since 1993  and will serve as Chairman of its Subcommittee on Agriculture  Rural Development  and FDA during the 110th Congress    Kohl also serves on the Subcommittees for Commerce  Justice  Science  Homeland Security  Interior and Environment  Labor  Health and Human Services and Education  and Transportation and Housing and Urban Development
