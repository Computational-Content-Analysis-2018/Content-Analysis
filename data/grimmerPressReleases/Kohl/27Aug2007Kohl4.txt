Statement of Senator Kohl on the Resignation of Attorney General Gonzales                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    August 27  2007    Phone   202  224 5653        Statement of Senator Kohl on the Resignation of Attorney General Gonzales     I am pleased that there will be an opportunity for new leadership at the Justice Department   Attorney General Gonzales has lost the confidence of people all across America and needs to be replaced   It is essential that his successor have more independence from the White House and always put the rule of law before politics
