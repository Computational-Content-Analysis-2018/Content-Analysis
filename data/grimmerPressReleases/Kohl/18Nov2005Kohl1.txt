KOHL SECURES  200 000 FOR THE CENTER FOR MANUFACTURING EXCELLENCE IN LaCROSSE                      Press Release      For Immediate Release    November 18  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        KOHL SECURES  200 000 FOR THE CENTER FOR MANUFACTURING EXCELLENCE IN LaCROSSE        WASHINGTON  D C  B U S  Senator Herb Kohl announced today that the Senate passed appropriations bill that funds the Department of Housing and Urban Development includes  200 000 for the construction of a Center for Manufacturing Excellence in LaCrosse  The Senate passed the Treasury  Transportation  Housing and Urban Development Appropriations bill conference report late this afternoon  Kohl is a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee and had identified this project as a priority for funding    The Center for Manufacturing Excellence wil use these funds to further develop the leading edge companies and entrepreneurs that make Wisconsin s business climate so electric   said Kohl   Investing these federal dollars in communities throughout Wisconsin will promote economic development while giving our residents a return on their tax dollars    The Center for Manufacturing Excellence will be a regionally focused and quick response manufacturing training and entrepreneur center  This Center will serve as a place for companies looking to use collaborative efforts to conduct training and or develop new product lines and bring such products to market  The federal funds will be used as part of the total funds needed to construct the Center for Manufacturing Excellence in the International Business Park   The Senate Treasury  Transportation  Housing and Urban Development appropriations bill now goes to the President to be signed into law
