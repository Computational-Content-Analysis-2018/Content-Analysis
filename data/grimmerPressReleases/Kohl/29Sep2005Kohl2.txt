Statement of U S  Senator Herb Kohl on the Confirmation of Judge John Roberts as Chief Justice of the Supreme Court                    Press Release      For Immediate Release    September 29  2005    Phone   202  224 5653        Statement of U S  Senator Herb Kohl on the Confirmation of Judge John Roberts as Chief Justice of the Supreme Court     I supported the nomination of Judge Roberts as Chief Justice because of his distinguished career as a lawyer and a judge   During a private meeting with him  as well as through four impressive days of testimony  Judge Roberts made clear that he will address each case on its merits and approach each argument with an open mind   He assured the Judiciary Committee that he will not be an activist  and that he will rarely  if ever  look to overturn precedent   Finally  Judge Roberts recognizes a right to privacy in the Constitution  and he understands that people have come to rely on it
