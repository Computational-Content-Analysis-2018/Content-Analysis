SEN. KYL CONGRATULATES ARIZONA STATE UNIVERSITY ON NAMING OF LAW SCHOOL IN HONOR OF JUSTICE O'CONNOR
  Sen. Jon Kyl (R-AZ) today issued the following statement on the naming of The Sandra Day O'Connor College of Law at Arizona State University: 
   "I congratulate Arizona State University for naming its law school after Justice Sandra Day O'Connor. In naming its law school after Justice O'Connor, Arizona State University honors a great Arizonan and one of America's most distinguished jurists. It also demonstrates our state's commitment to turning young men and women into legal professionals who will live up to the inscription on the East Pediment of the Court that Sandra Day O'Connor served so well for 25 years: 'Justice, the Guardian of Liberty.'"
