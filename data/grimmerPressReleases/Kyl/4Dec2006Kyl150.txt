SEN. KYL ISSUES STATEMENT ON RESIGNATION OF AMBASSADOR JOHN BOLTON
Sen. Jon Kyl (R-AZ) today issued the following statement regarding the announced resignation of John Bolton, U.S. Ambassador to the United Nations, effective at the end of his recess appointment. 
 "I commend Ambassador Bolton for representing United States interests with great aplomb during such a critical time. It's a shame that a handful of senators can thwart his confirmation, which is so needed right now."
