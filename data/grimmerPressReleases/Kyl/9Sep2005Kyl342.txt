SEN. KYL'S WEB SITE OFFERS GUIDANCE ON HELPING VICTIMS OF KATRINA
  Sen. Kyl today announced that those seeking to contribute to the disaster relief effort along the Gulf Coast can obtain extensive information on how to do so on his web site. 
   "Americans in general and Arizonans in particular are generous people, quick to help out others in need, across the country and the world," Kyl said. "For those who want to help but don't know how, my office has compiled a list of options." 
   The list of organizations accepting cash and other donations is available at kyl.senate.gov/katrina.
