SEN. KYL ISSUES STATEMENT ON CIA DIRECTOR TENET'S RESIGNATION
  Sen. Jon Kyl (R-AZ), a former member of the Senate Select Committee on Intelligence, today released the following statement on the resignation announcement of CIA Director George Tenet:
   "Running the CIA is a tough job. We appreciate George Tenet's service to his country and look forward to a new start."
