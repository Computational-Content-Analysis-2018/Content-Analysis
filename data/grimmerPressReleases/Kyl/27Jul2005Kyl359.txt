SEN. KYL MEETS WITH JUDGE ROBERTS
  Sen. Jon Kyl today met with Supreme Court nominee Judge John Roberts, calling their discussion "useful and illuminating." 
   "One can't help but be impressed by both the man and the credentials he bears," Kyl said after the meeting. "He's very impressive and clearly has the background, temperament and potential to be a solid addition to the Supreme Court." 
   Kyl added that, despite interest group criticism of the nomination, he remains hopeful that the confirmation process can be conducted in a dignified atmosphere and concluded by October 3.
