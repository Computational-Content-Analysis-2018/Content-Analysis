                                                                                                            Klobuchar Joins UMD Women s Hockey Team at White House Ceremony                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  NCAA champion Bulldogs honored at event                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          June 24  2008 Washington  D C   At a White House ceremony today  U S   joined members of the University of Minnesota Duluth Womens Bulldog Hockey Team and congratulated them for winning the 2007 2008 NCAA championship   The Bulldogs didnt just bring home a national championship  they have brought Minnesota an incredible amount of energy and pride  Klobuchar said  Their record  four national championships in eight years  speaks for itself and Im proud to represent such a remarkable group of young women    The defending national champion UMD Bulldogs were making their fourth White House trip in the past decade  From 2001 2003 they racked up three national titles  Led by coach Shannon Miller  the Bulldogs posted a record of 43 4 1 and beat the University of Wisconsin 4 0 for the 2007 2008 Division 1 National Championship
