                                                                                                            Klobuchar Backs Frank Magill as Acting U S  Attorney                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Department of Justice appointment consistent with Senator s recommendation to appoint internal candidate                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         January 3  2008 Washington  D C   U S   today released the following statement regarding the Department of Justices appointment of First Assistant U S  Attorney Frank Magill as Acting U S  Attorney for the District of Minnesota  I have worked with Frank  both when we were prosecutors and in private practice  I consider him a friend  and have great respect for him and his work  Hes a true professional  and a great choice to lead this office  Senator Klobuchar served as Hennepin County Attorney from 1998 to 2006 before being elected to serve Minnesota in the U S  Senate  As County Attorney  Klobuchar aggressively prosecuted white collar financial crimes  working extensively with the U S  Attorneys office while Magill headed the economic crimes division  Magill has served as First Assistant U S  Attorney since June 2007  Prior to that  he served as assistant U S  Attorney since 1990  serving as the economic crimes chief from 1998 to June 2007  Magill received his bachelors degree from Georgetown University in 1981 and graduated from Georgetown Law magna cum laude in 1985
