                                                                                                            Klobuchar Secures Over  23 Million for Minnesota National Guard in Omnibus Appropriations Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Bill includes  17 45 million for Minnesota National Guard Infrastructure Project  Klobuchar Continues to fight for the needs of Minnesota s veterans and National Guard December 17  2007 Washington  D C   As part of her commitment to fully support Minnesotas National Guard    D MN  secured over  23 million in military infrastructure projects for Minnesota  including  17 45 million to construct a Combined Arms Collective Training Facility at Camp Ripley  The facility will provide an urban combat training environment for soldiers preparing for deployment and serve as a critical training center for the Minnesota National Guard as well as other military and law enforcement units across the Midwest  The projects are included in the 2008 Omnibus Appropriations bill which includes funding Klobuchar secured as part of H R  2642  the 2008 Appropriations bill for Military Construction  Department of Veterans Affairs  and Related Agencies  Our Minnesota National Guard continues to serve us all proudly both at home and abroad  said Klobuchar  These brave men and women are always there when we need them most and projects like these make sure they have the resources and training they need to do their job  The site will provide a Joint Force Headquarters for the Minnesota National Guard  and a Joint Emergency Operations Center for the State of Minnesota and Homeland Security Emergency Management  HSEM   The new operations center provides permanent office space for up to 70 federal and state agency employees to work in a secure environment  The open source portion of the center supports up to 100 community or non profit partners to respond to emergencies  Senator Klobuchar has remained steadfast in her support of Minnesotas National Guard  She has called for more equitable benefits for deployed guard members  requesting additional federal support for Minnesotas innovative Beyond the Yellow Ribbon program  which helps returning Guard troops with the transition back to their families and local communities  In addition to the Arden Hills facility  Klobuchar secured the following funding as a part of the 2008 Appropriations bill for Military Construction  Department of Veterans Affairs  and Related Agencies bill which is included in the Omnibus legislation   3 5 million for the construction of a Joint Force Headquarters and Emergency Operations Center at Arden Hills Army Training Site  Arden Hills    1 366 million for a field maintenance shop  Mankato   1 5 million fora wing storage facility for the 148th Fighter Wing Duluth
