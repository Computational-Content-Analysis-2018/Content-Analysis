                                                                                                            Klobuchar Statement on Fed Railroad Association s Decision for DM Rail Plan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     January 31  2007  Washington  D C   U S   tonight released the following statement in reaction to the Federal Railroad Association s  FRA  decision stating that the Dakota  Minnesota and Eastern  DME  Railroad Corporation s project has met the requirements of the federal environmental review  This decision starts a 90 day clock in which a final decision on DME s loan application must be made  As the Department of Transportation approaches a final decision on this loan  it must confront very serious questions about the loan application  and it must make sure that dozens of high speed freight trains do not run right through the heart of Rochester and the Mayo Clinic
