                                                                                                            Sen  Klobuchar Offers Support to People of Burma                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  A Tragedy Not Just for the People of Burma  But for All of Us                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   May 6  2008 WASHINGTON  DC  U S   today issued the following statement about the cyclone disaster in Burma   On behalf of Minnesota  I extend my sympathy and support to the people of Burma in the aftermath of the deadly cyclone that devastated their country over the weekend   When a disaster of this terrible scale occurs  it is a tragedy not just for the people of Burma  but for all of us  It is of special significance to Minnesota  because we are home to thousands of people from Burma  including the largest U S  concentration of Karen refugees who have been victims of religious and ethnic persecution under Burmas military regime    Burma is a country whose people have already suffered greatly under a repressive dictatorship  For the safety and health of its people  I urge the government of Burma to accept the many offers of aid and assistance from the United States  the United Nations and countries around the world
