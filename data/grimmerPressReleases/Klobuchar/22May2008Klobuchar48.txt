                                                                                                            Klobuchar Support Helps Pass Amendment to Protect Minnesota s Recreational Boaters                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Measure ensures Minnesota s boaters aren t burdened with paperwork requirements                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  May 22  2008 Washington  D C   The Environment and Public Works Committee passed legislation yesterday that U S   cosponsored to exempt Minnesotas recreational boaters from being burdened with additional permit requirements under federal water regulations  The legislation would ensure that new standards on boat discharge from bilge and engine fluids would not require small boat owners to apply for cumbersome permits    In the land of 10 000 lakes  there is no more precious resource than our water  We must protect our lakes  but we can and must do it without hurting our boaters  Klobuchar said  This legislation ensures that recreational boaters all across the state can focus on fishing and having fun  not new regulations     The Clean Boating Act now moves to the full Senate for consideration  Klobuchar serves on both the Senate Commerce Committee and Senate Environment and Public Works Committee
