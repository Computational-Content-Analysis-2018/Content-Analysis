                                                                                                            Klobuchar Helps Win Senate Passage of Bill to Protect Minnesota s Recreational Boaters                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Measure frees Minnesota boaters from burdensome paperwork requirements                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           July 25  2008 Washington  D C   The Senate today adopted legislation cosponsored by U S   to protect Minnesotas recreational boaters from being burdened with additional permit requirements under federal water regulations  The Clean Boating Act of 2008 would ensure that broad federal standards on boat discharges  such as bilge water and engine fluids  will not require small boat owners to apply for cumbersome federal permits   Minnesota is the Land of 10 000 Lakes  a state where we protect our water and other natural resources  But we can do it without placing unnecessary burdens on the family boater  Klobuchar said  This legislation ensures that recreational boaters all across the state can focus on fishing and having fun  not new regulations     The Clean Boating Act of 2008 protects a 35 year old exemption so that incidental discharges from small recreational boats  such as deck runoff and engine cooling water  do not require the same major federal permits required of larger commercial vessels  It also requires the U S  Environmental Protection Agency to develop reasonable and practical guidelines for recreational boaters to protect water from engine discharges    The measure passed the Senate this week by unanimous consent
