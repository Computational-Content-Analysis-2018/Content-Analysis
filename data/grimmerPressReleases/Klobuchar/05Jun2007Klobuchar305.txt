                                                                                                            Klobuchar Issues Statement on Senator Craig Thomas                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 June 5  2007  Washington  D C   Senator Klobuchar issued the following statement on the passing of Senator Craig Thomas  R WY   I am deeply saddened by the death of Wyoming Senator Craig Thomas  Senator Thomas was an honorable public servant who worked valiantly for the people of Wyoming  I am grateful for the opportunity I had to work with him on the Environmental and Public Works Committee  EPW   My thoughts and prayers are with his wife and family
