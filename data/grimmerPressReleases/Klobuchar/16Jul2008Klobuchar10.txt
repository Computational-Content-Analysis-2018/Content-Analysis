                                                                                                            Klobuchar Announces  84 000 Veterans Affairs Grant for St  Cloud Salvation Army                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Grant will support expansion of emergency shelter program                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        July 16  2008 Washington  D C   U S   announced today that the Salvation Army of St  Cloud will receive  84 315 from the Department of Veterans Affairs to expand its transitional housing shelter    The grant was made under the departments Homeless Providers grant program    This is a nation that wraps our arms around our veterans  Klobuchar said  When they go into combat  they risk their lives for our freedom and security  and when they come home they deserve the very best services we can give them  This grant will help the Salvation Army provide those services    The St  Cloud Salvation Army operates several programs for those in need  including transitional emergency housing and a community lunch program    Klobuchar has been a consistent champion of veterans issues  cosponsoring legislation to improve their education benefits  provide high quality health care and streamline training programs for veterans to earn certification as Emergency Medical Technicians
