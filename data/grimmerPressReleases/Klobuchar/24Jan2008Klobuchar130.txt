                                                                                                            Klobuchar Calls for Stimulus Package with Long term Investments in Jobs and Energy                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Welcomes bipartisan progress this week                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           January 24  2008 Washington  D C    welcomed the tentative bipartisan agreement announced Thursday on a plan to stimulate the faltering economy  but said she wants the final package to include provisions that will encourage long term economic growth  smart energy and infrastructure investments  and the creation of good middle class jobs  Average American families have seen very little benefit from the economic expansion of the last seven years and have been first to feel the effects of the mortgage crisis  higher fuel costs and other shocks to the economy  so its imperative that we pass a stimulus package that helps them through this difficult period  Klobuchar said  But its equally important that we make long term investments in our economy that produce good jobs and economic benefits long after the  300 checks are spent  Klobuchar  who serves on the Joint Economic Committee  said she would push to include investments in needed infrastructure  such as roads and bridges  and alternative energy technology as work on the package continues  This month in Maple Grove Klobuchar unveiled the American Renewable Energy Act  which is designed to stimulate investment in renewable energy sources such as wind and solar power by extending the federal Production Tax Credit and by setting aggressive Federal standards for use of renewable energy sources
