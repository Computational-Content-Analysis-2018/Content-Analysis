                                                                                                            Sen  Klobuchar Statement on Announcement by U S  Attorney Rachel Paulose                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           November 19  2007 Washington  D C   U S   released the following statement after learning that Minnesota U S  Attorney Rachel Paulose accepted a new position at the United States Justice Department in Washington  D C   As Hennepin County prosecutor I worked closely with the Minnesota US Attorney s Office for eight years  I have always been impressed by the dedicated prosecutors in the US Attorney s Office and I have been very concerned with the turmoil in the office over the past year  I am pleased that the Minnesota US Attorney s Office is moving forward under new leadership  I hope this will be the last chapter for our state when it comes to the legacy left by Alberto Gonzales
