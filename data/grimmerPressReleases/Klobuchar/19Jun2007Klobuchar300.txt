                                                                                                            Sen  Klobuchar Statement on Minnesota National Guard Homecoming                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    June 19  2007  Washington  D C   Senator Klobuchar released the following statement today in response to members of the Minnesota National Guard 1st Brigade Combat Team returning from Iraq  I join all Minnesotans in welcoming the return of our National Guard soldiers from the 1st Brigade Combat Team  When a soldier returns home  it is also a homecoming for the family  for the local community and for our whole state  This homecoming is all the more heartfelt because these troops were expected to return many months ago until their tour of duty was suddenly extended
