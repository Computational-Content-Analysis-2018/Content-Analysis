                                                                                                            Sen  Klobuchar s Statement on the Retirement of U S  Representative Jim Ramstad                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    September 17  2007 Washington  D C   U S   released the following statement regarding veteran Congressman Jim Ramstads announcement that he will retire from the U S  House of Representatives after serving Minnesotas Third District for nine terms  Jim Ramstad is a person of great integrity  who always does what he thinks is right for his constituents rather than simply following the party line  He fostered a spirit of bi partisanship in Minnesota and in Washington  Jim is an exemplary public servant  and he will be missed in the halls of Congress  Ive enjoyed working with Jim  from this years pool safety legislation  to our many public safety initiatives during my time as Hennepin County Attorney  In particular  his dedication to the Wellstone Mental Health and Addiction Equity Act is a testament to both his willingness to work across the aisle to advance important issues and to his special friendship with Senator Wellstone
