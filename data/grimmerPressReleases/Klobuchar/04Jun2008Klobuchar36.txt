                                                                                                            Klobuchar  Hwy  43 Bridge Closing Shows Need for Improved Infrastructure Investment                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                June 4  2008 Washington  D C   U S   issued the following statement Wednesday on the closing of the Hwy  43 bridge at Winona    The safety of our citizens should be paramount  and closing the Hwy  43 bridge was the only adequate response to clear signs of decay in our state transportation system  This closing is one more sign that infrastructure maintenance in our state  and across the nation   has fallen behind the needs of our communities and the requirements of basic safety    I will continue to work with state and local officials to address the immediate needs of area businesses and residents  and I will continue my fight for increased investment in our nations aging infrastructure
