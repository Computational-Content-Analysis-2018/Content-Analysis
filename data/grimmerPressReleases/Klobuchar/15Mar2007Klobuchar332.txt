                                                                                                             Statement on Senate Iraq Resolution to Change Course in Iraq                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 March 15  2007  Washington  D C   U S   today issued the following statement after voting on the Iraq resolution calling for a revision in U S  policy toward Iraq  The message I ve heard all across Minnesota is that we need a change of course in Iraq  The resolution I voted for today  much like the bipartisan Iraq Study Group s recommendations  calls for a phased redeployment of our combat troops out of Iraq  It s disappointing that while Iraq is mired in a civil war  and our own Minnesota National Guard troops are having their tours extended  some Senators continue to support an open ended  stay the course strategy  After four years  this open ended commitment does not serve the interests of our troops or the Iraqi people  who must stand up and forge the necessary political solutions
