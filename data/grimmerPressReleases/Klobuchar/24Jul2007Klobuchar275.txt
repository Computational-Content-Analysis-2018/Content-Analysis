                                                                                                            Senator Klobuchar Statement on Minimum Wage as New Law Takes Effect                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               July 24  2007 Washington  D C   Celebrating the Democratic Congress achievement of increasing the minimum wage    D MN  joined over     House members and over    Senators  at a rally in Upper Senate Park  directly across the street from the Capitol  Senator Klobuchar issued the following statement following todays event    The minimum wage is about helping working families  it is fair  it is smart  and above all it is right  said Klobuchar  Our lowest paid workers have seen the purchasing power of their wages decline even as their health care  housing  education  and energy costs skyrocket  and it is long past time that we help them make ends meet  Today we can finally tell our workers that we stand up for the hardworking people of America
