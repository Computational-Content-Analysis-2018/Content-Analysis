                                                                                                            Statement of  Regarding Deferred Enforced Departure for Liberians                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             September 12  2007 Washington  D C     offered the following statement tonight on the granting of Deferred Enforced Departure for Liberians in the United States    The Liberian Community has become an important part of the social fabric of Minnesota   they are our neighbors and our co workers  While Liberia continues to be unstable  it is important that our Liberian community is able to continue to call Minnesota home  This 18 month extension will allow us to continue our pursuit of a more permanent solution    Klobuchar is already a co sponsor of a Senate bill addressing the residency status of Liberians currently in the United States
