                                                                                                            Klobuchar Statement on Bush Farm Bill Veto                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         May 21  2008 Washington  D C   U S   released the following statement regarding the Presidents veto of the 2008 Farm Bill    This bill is vital to provide our farmers and ranchers the support they need  We cannot wait any longer to get it done  We have a strong  bipartisan bill  thanks to the leadership of Collin Peterson and Tom Harkin  It includes a significant step toward payment reform  makes the improvements in the safety net for sugar and dairy producers that I have advocated for  and invests in landmark renewable energy initiatives  while providing for the critical nutrition and conservation programs we need    In the coming years  I will continue to fight for our working farmers and ranchers to ensure they can provide America with a safe  abundant food supply and sources of renewable energy  I will also continue to push for additional payment reforms to ensure that taxpayer dollars are spent wisely and go to our hardworking farmers  not urban millionaires  I am confident that we will be able to override the Presidents veto    U S   serves on the Agriculture  Nutrition and Forestry Committee
