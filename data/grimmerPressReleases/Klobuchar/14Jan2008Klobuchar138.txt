                                                                                                            Klobuchar Statement on Northwest Airlines                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Klobuchar  Member of the Senate Commerce Aviation Subcommittee  expresses concerns to Northwest CEO Doug Steenland about potential merger                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        January 14  2008 Washington  D C   U S   issued the following statement about any potential merger after speaking with Northwest CEO Doug Steenland yesterday  Klobuchar serves on the Senate Commerce Committee and its Subcommittee on Aviation Operations  Safety  and Security which have jurisdiction over airline operations  Klobuchar highlighted Northwests importance to Minnesota stating  I spoke with Doug Steenland yesterday to emphasize the need for the airlines continued presence and investment in the economic growth of Minnesota and a connection to the global economy  The jobs and economic development associated with the airline are a driving force behind Minnesotas economy  ranging from its operations in Eagan to Chisholm to Rochester  Minnesotas position as a hub for Northwest allows us to be a commercial center for small businesses and Fortune 500 companies alike  bringing thousands of visitors to our state each year  Any transaction relating to the airline industry must be examined carefully and thoroughly with an eye towards ensuring that the economic health of our state and its workers is not jeopardized  Early last year  Senator Klobuchar played an active role in the Senate Commerce Committees hearings regarding the possible merger of U S  Airways and Delta
