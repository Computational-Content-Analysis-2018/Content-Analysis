       Releases                                                                                                                                           10 05 2005 John Kerry on Senate Defeat of LIHEAP Amendment                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Below is a statement by Senator John Kerry  D Mass   on the Senate   s defeat of an amendment that would fully fund the Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program  LIHEAP   The amendment was defeated by a point of order that blocked an up or down vote tonight                                                                                                                                                       It   s hard to believe that the Republican leadership would stand in the way of emergency funding to help millions of families and seniors heat their homes this winter   By blocking the LIHEAP amendment on procedural vote  the Republican leadership has sent a terrible message to Americans because tonight politics was placed ahead of helping families   It   s the wrong choices in Washington that have put us in this energy crisis  and now it   s politics as usual standing in the way of fixing this mess   American families deserve more from their leaders in Washington than this   this time they are very literally being left out in the cold   If we   ve learned anything from the past months it   s that we need to prepare for trouble ahead   With the defeat of this amendment  we remain unprepared for what may be a long  cold and expensive winter for millions of American families                                                                                       20510
