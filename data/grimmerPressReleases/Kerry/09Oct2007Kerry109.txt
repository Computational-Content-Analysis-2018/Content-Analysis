       Releases                                                                                                                                           10 09 2007 Kerry congratulates two Massachusetts educators for winning national awards for teaching                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               BOSTON  Senator John Kerry today congratulated two Massachusetts educators for winning national awards for teaching    Anne Clark who teaches English at the Boston Arts Academy and Matthew Dugan  who teaches science at Madison Park Technical High School in Boston  are both recipients of the Milken Family Foundation National Educator Award    Anne Clark and Matthew Dugans excellence in the classroom and commitment to their students is extraordinary  Day in and day out they find new and exciting ways to make learning fun  said Senator Kerry  On behalf of all of Massachusetts  I thank them for their dedication to our youth and to the ensuring the future of state    Both Clark and Dugan will receive  25 000 bonuses along with the award  They are just two of 80 educators across the nation who are receiving the award    The Milken Family Foundation National Educator Awards program provides public recognition and financial rewards to elementary and secondary schoolteachers  principals and other education professionals who are furthering excellence in education     More than over 2 300 educators across the country have been recognized since the Awards inception
