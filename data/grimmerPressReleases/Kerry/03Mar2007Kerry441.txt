       Releases                                                                                                                                           03 03 2007 Father Robert Drinan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Senator Kerry attended a memorial service for his close friend and mentor Father Robert Drinan at St  Ignatius Church at Boston College  Senator Kerry and Senator Kennedy then introduced a resolution honoring Father Drinan in the Senate  which passed unanimously  Father Drinan was a gentle  resilient  tenacious advocate for social justice and fundamental decency  said Senator Kerry  In the most divisive days of the Vietnam War  Drinan showed America how a man of faith could impact our nation not just spiritually  but politically  Its appropriate that we honor him in Congress  where he served with such conviction  and continues to be an example to all of us  All of us who knew Father Drinan and served with him admired him for his deep faith  his profound commitment to public service  and the bold actions he constantly urged us to take to live up to our principles  especially in ending the Vietnam War  The nation has lost one of the finest persons ever to serve in Congress                                                                          20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
