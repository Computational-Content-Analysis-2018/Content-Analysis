       Releases                                                                                                                                           09 19 2007 Kerry  Kennedy congratulate Children s Museum on  420 000 grant                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Boston  Senators John Kerry and Edward Kennedy today congratulated the Boston Children s Museum on receiving a  423 214 grant from the Institute of Museum and Library Services  IMLS  under the 21st Century Museum Professionals Program  In partnership with the Chicago Children s Museum  the Boston Children s Museum will incorporate family learning research into staff training in order to create more effective learning environments for multigenerational families  The new staff training methods developed at the Boston and Chicago museums are intended to lead to a new national model for enhancing the museum experience of multigenerational families and will be broadly disseminated through professional associations and national conference workshops  Generations of Massachusetts children have taken their first steps towards to becoming engineers  marine biologists  graphic designers  doctors and inventors during a visit to the Boston Children s Museum  Senator Kerry said  It s vitally important to instill in children that imagination  exploration and intellectual curiosity are very good things and that s exactly what the talented and dedicated people at the Children s Museum have been successfully doing for decades  I commend the IMLS for investing in a true Massachusetts institution  Senator Kennedy said  I commend the Boston Childrens Museum for all they do to improve learning for children and families in the Commonwealth  This federal funding is well deserved                                                                                20510
