       Releases                                                                                                                                           02 27 2006 Senator John Kerry to Speak on Security in Northern Ireland                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Senator John Kerry  a senior member of the Senate Foreign Relations Committee  will visit Northern Ireland on March 5 to deliver a keynote lecture at the University of Ulster       Kerry   s speech will focus on    Security in the 21st Century    and is part of the Tip O   Neill series of peace lectures        Kerry was invited to speak by Professor John Hume  holder of the Tip O   Neill Chair in Peace Studies at the University   The Tip O   Neill Chair  funded by the Ireland Funds  commemorates the former Speaker of the House who was well known for his support of the peace process in Northern Ireland     Others to have delivered lectures in the series include former President Bill Clinton  Taoiseach Bertie Ahern  President of the European Commission Romano Prodi  and former Taoiseach Garret FitzGerald
