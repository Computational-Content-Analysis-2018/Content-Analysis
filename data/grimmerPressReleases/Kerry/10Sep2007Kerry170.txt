       Releases                                                                                                                                           09 10 2007 Kerry on death of Paul Sullivan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        BOSTON  Senator John Kerry today released the following statement on the death of Paul Sullivan      Paul was balanced  honest  fair and civil public discourse at its best  Well always remember him as a gentleman first  a journalist second  He cared about the people covered and the personalities he interviewed  and it showed       Throughout his very public illness Paul was always dignified  and he approached it with his trademark sense of humor  Despite four surgeries  you didnt hear him complain  He resisted the most well intended efforts to turn him into a hero  he just wanted to be the same Paul Sullivan hed been his whole life  the genuine article      Teresa and I send our thoughts and prayers to his wife Mary Jo  his five children and everyone who knew and loved him  His family and friends can take some comfort in knowing how many people he touched in the fifty years he was with us  He will be deeply missed                                                                                  20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
