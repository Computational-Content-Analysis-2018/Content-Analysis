       Releases                                                                                                                                           03 02 2006 Statement by Senator John Kerry  D Mass   on LIHEAP Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Below is a statement by Senator John Kerry  D Mass   on today   s vote in the Senate to block a bill by Senator Olympia Snowe  R ME  that would have freed up  1 billion from the recently enacted Budget Reconciliation Act  and make them available in FY2006  The Senate blocked the bill tonight on a budget point of order          Since October my colleagues and I have fought to fully fund LIHEAP so our families and seniors don   t have to worry about getting food on the table or go without medical care just so they can heat their homes   This is a very real choice that too many Americans have had to make this winter  LIHEAP is a commonsense solution to this very real problem of high home heating costs     a problem that literally leaves millions of families out in the cold   It   s almost unbelievable that this Republican leadership would block Senator Snowe   s effort to get this funding to our families before it   s too late  Some members of this Senate know that issues like fully funding LIHEAP shouldn   t be held up by typical party politics  It   s too bad the leaders of our Senate can   t see that                                                                                    20510
