       Releases                                                                                                                                           09 10 2007 Kerry to Speak at Dedication of Massachusetts Fallen Firefighters Memorial on Tuesday                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  BOSTON  Senator John Kerry will speak at the unveiling and dedication of the Massachusetts Fallen Firefighters Memorial at the State House on Tuesday    The Massachusetts Fallen Firefighters Memorial is a long overdue tribute to our states brave firefighters who lost their lives in the line of duty  Dedicated on the sixth anniversary of 9 11 when so many firefighters died so others could live  this memorial is a reminder that no one should never take for granted the service of these incredible public servants  said Senator Kerry  I am honored to be part of the dedication ceremony  and I hope this memorial provides some small reminder to firefighters and the families of those weve lost of just how grateful we are for their sacrifice    WHO  Senator John Kerry   WHAT  Unveiling and Dedication of Massachusetts Fallen Firefighters Memorial   WHEN  Tuesday  September 11  2007 6 00 pm     WHERE  Ashburton Park at the State House Boston                                                                                 20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
