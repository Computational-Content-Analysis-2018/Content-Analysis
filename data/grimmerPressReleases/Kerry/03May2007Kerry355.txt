       Releases                                                                                                                                           05 03 2007 Kerry Says Secretary Rice Syria Meeting Is Overdue                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON D C    Senator John Kerry  D Mass   issued the following statement in response to Secretary of State Rice s meeting with the Syrian Foreign Minister today at the Iraq summit in Egypt  Kerry  who traveled to Syria on a fact finding mission in December and met with President Assad  has long called on the Administration to engage in direct dialogue with Syria         Secretary Rice s long overdue meeting with the Syrian Foreign Minister is welcome news to many from both parties who have called for direct engagement with Syria   Senator Kerry said    I appreciate the fact that this Administration  which has been so quick to criticize those of us who have met with Syrian leaders  has finally decided to take this important diplomatic step   There s no better way to test Syrian intentions than by asking tough questions face to face  I hope the White House uses this opportunity to engage in meaningful dialogue with Syria as the United States and the international community works towards our goal of stabilizing Iraq and the region                                                                           20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
