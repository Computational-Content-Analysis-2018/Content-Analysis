       Releases                                                                                                                                           08 03 2007 Kerry Says Bush Climate Conference is Useless without Mandatory Caps on Emissions                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 September meeting will be  more of the same                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         WASHINGTON D C    Sen  John Kerry issued the following statement today  in response to the White House announcement this morning that President Bush will convene a climate change conference in September with 15 countries and the United Nations  Although the stated goal of reducing greenhouse gases is a good one  Kerry pointed out that Bush has repeatedly refused to agree to any serious reductions in emissions and has actively worked to subvert government efforts to reduce pollution         It d be great if this meeting was actually going to produce some positive outcomes but I fear it will be more of the same   Kerry said   There are dozens of ways this administration could take productive steps on climate change  but simply restating goals without taking the steps necessary to implement them is a recipe for failure  We need binding emissions targets across the economy and across borders  Our only hope is that this president  who just a few years ago was questioning the science behind climate change  may wake up one day and finally decide he is ready to lead on this critical issue                                                                           20510
