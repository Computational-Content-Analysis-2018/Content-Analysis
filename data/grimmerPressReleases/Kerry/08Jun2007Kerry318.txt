       Releases                                                                                                                                           06 08 2007 Kerry Congratulates Lowell Police Officer for being Named Officer of the Month by the National Law Enforcement Officers Memorial Fund                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 BOSTON  Senator John Kerry today congratulated Detective Bryan McMahon of the Lowell Police Department for being awarded Officer of the Month by the National Law Enforcement Officers Memorial Fund  McMahon was recognized for his work on behalf of fallen comrades as well as of the children of police officers killed in the line of duty  Detective McMahon embodies what it means to be a hero  Both on and off duty he has demonstrated flawless integrity  compassion  and utter professionalism  His tireless work with survivors of those lost in the line of duty is inspiring to all know him  said Senator Kerry  I commend Bryan for receiving this honor and thank him for 30 years of dedication to the city of Lowell and to the state of Massachusetts  McMahon  a 30 year veteran of Lowells Police Department  also currently is the President of the New England Police Benevolent Association  Inc  I U P A  Local 9000  AFL CIO and is a national representative for the International Brotherhood of Police Officers  The National Law Enforcement Officer Memorial Fund is a non profit organization dedicated to honoring the service and sacrifice of Americas law enforcement officers  Its Officer of the Month Program  which began in 1997  recognizes federal  state and local officers who distinguish themselves through exemplary law enforcement service and devotion to duty McMahon  along with the other national winners  will be honored in Washington  DC in 2008                                                                              20510
