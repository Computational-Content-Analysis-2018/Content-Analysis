       Releases                                                                                                                                           03 12 2007 Kerry  I doubt ICE has any real idea about who it s detaining                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         BOSTON   Senator John Kerry today issued the following statement on New Bedford parents detained in Texas who  despite assurances from officials  still haven t been reunited with their children  Every day this story gets worse  When I spoke to Secretary Chertoff last Thursday night  he assured me that no parents were being detained in Texas  Now we learn of the discovery of nineteen sole or primary caretakers of children  one minor child  and one woman suffering from cancer at the Texas detention facility  It is unacceptable that these people were flown half way across the country before this information was discovered  I have serious doubts as to whether ICE has any real idea about who they are detaining and where  I am just grateful that they eventually granted the Massachusetts Department of Social Services access to the Texas facility  Every effort must be made to reunify these families immediately  As Mayor Lang has requested  all immigrants should be returned to New Bedford to make this happen  After a week of disastrous decisions and poor planning  the Office of Immigration and Customs Enforcement needs to do something to help the families that have been ripped apart
