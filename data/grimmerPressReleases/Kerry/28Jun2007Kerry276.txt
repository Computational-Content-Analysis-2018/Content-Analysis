       Releases                                                                                                                                           06 28 2007 Kerry Congratulates Marlborough Company for being named one of the country s top performing small businesses                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           BOSTON  Senator John Kerry  Chairman of the Senate Committee on Small Business and Entrepreneurship today announced that Digital Federal Credit Union of Marlborough has been named on of the Top 50 Small  Medium Companies to Work for in America in the U S  by the Society for Human Resource Management  SHRM  and the Great Places to Work Institute    Digital Federal Credit Union has been a leader in creating a caring  productive work environment  said Senator Kerry  I am thrilled that it has been recognized for its innovation and I wish it success in continuing to promote a great work environment while also caring for its customers    Digital Federal Credit Union prides itself on understanding and motivating their employees by granting them paid maternity leave  inviting them involved in corporate planning  and keeping them abreast of the latest company news    The SHRM s list  which is in its fourth year  identifies 25 small and 25 medium sized companies that use successful management strategies and have highly productive  satisfied staffs  Each company was evaluated via employee responses to questions about motivation and workplace culture  Digital Federal Credit Union was one of only three Massachusetts businesses granted a place on the list                                                                          20510
