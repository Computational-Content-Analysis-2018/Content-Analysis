       Releases                                                                                                                                           10 24 2007 Kerry on Coast Guard s recommendation against LNG facility in Fall River                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               BOSTON  Senator John Kerry today issued the following statement about the Coast Guards recommendation against a Liquefied Natural Gas  LNG  facility in Fall River  The Coast Guard reached the conclusion  after exhaustive study  that the waterway is unsuitable for LNG traffic due to navigational safety challenges and that the risk of mishap is unacceptably high    This latest ruling could mean victory for the people of Fall River and folks up and down the Narragansett and Mount Hope Bays who have battled to stop this terminal   The Coast Guard has spoken in favor of common sense and public safety  These huge tankers couldve invited a disaster  Its great news that the Coast Guard realizes these dangers  Weve said it before  and well say it again   this LNG terminal should never have been proposed  should never have gone forward  and Senator Kennedy  Congressman Frank and I will continue to do everything in our power to make sure this dangerous proposal is defeated once and for all  said Senator Kerry                                                                          20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
