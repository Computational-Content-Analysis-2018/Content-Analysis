       Releases                                                                                                                                           07 19 2007 Kerry on Patrick s plan to keep Massachusetts a global leader in life sciences industry                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                BOSTON  Senator John Kerry today issued the following statement on Governor Deval Patricks legislation which would provide for a major investment in the life science industry in Massachusetts   Governor Patrick has stepped forward with a bold  courageous plan that will keep Massachusetts in the lead in life sciences research for the next decade and beyond  This investment promises new and innovative discoveries  Dr  Craig Mello and his colleagues in Massachusetts are already on the cutting edge of dozens of life saving technologies  This commitment from the state will empower them to build on those advances and the potential for hundreds of thousands of Americans to one day benefit from their discoveries   Governor Patricks leadership is refreshing  We must still overcome partisan  ideological gridlock in Washington to force the federal government to accept that ethical stem cell therapy can potentially cure disease and save lives  Americans have been presented with a false choice between honoring human life and promoting the science and research that can save it  We can have both  and we will have both  and Governor Patricks investment is a down payment on proving its possible
