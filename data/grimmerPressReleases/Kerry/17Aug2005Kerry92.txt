       Releases                                                                                                                                           08 17 2005 SENATOR KERRY  SENATOR KENNEDY ANNOUNCE GRANT FOR MEDFORD LAW ENFORCEMENT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Today Senators John Kerry and Edward Kennedy announced a  12 978 grant for the Medford Police Department to fund overtime costs and officer training to protect the city s young people and keep gangs out of the city         Senator Kerry said     The men and women of the Medford Police Department work everyday to keep our neighborhoods and our families safe   It s important that officers remain a visible presence in the community  and they are certainly committed to doing that     Senator Kerry said       Senator Kennedy said   The police officers of Medford are doing an outstanding job of increasing their presence among the city s youth to prevent crime  It s an effective strategy to reduce crime  and the funds will enable the Department to continue this essential work and give these officers the overtime pay they deserve          The Edward Byrne Memorial Justice Assistance Grant  JAG  program aims to prevent and control crime  JAG funds can be used to aid programs that concentrate on law enforcement  prosecution and courts  prevention and education  corrections and community corrections  drug treatment  and technology improvement            30
