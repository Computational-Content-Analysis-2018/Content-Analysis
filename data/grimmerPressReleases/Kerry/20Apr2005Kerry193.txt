       Releases                                                                                                                                           04 20 2005 John Kerry on Retirement of Senator Jim Jeffords                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Below is a statement from Senator John Kerry on Senator Jim Jeffords     I Vt   announcement that he will not seek reelection                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            This quiet  thoughtful man embodies true political courage   Jim Jeffords put everything on the line acting out of deep personal conviction when he declared his independence  and endured the personal attacks that followed with grace and dignity   As a passionate advocate for children  a committed environmentalist  and a guardian of fiscal responsibility  Jim has always followed his own moral compass         Jim always places people above politics  and the Senate will lose a true statesman with his retirement         A remarkable person and public servant  I wish Jim all the best and look forward to working with him for the remainder of his term   His wife Liz remains in our thoughts as she brings her own brand of courage to the fight she   s waging against cancer
