       Releases                                                                                                                                           05 30 2007 Kerry Says Zoellick Must Reach Out to World Community                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  WASHINGTON  DC   Sen  John Kerry  D Mass  issued the following statement today  in response to President Bush s decision to nominate Goldman Sachs banker Robert Zoellick to replace Paul Wolfowitz as head of the World Bank         The World Bank desperately needs a leader who can regain the confidence of employees and donor nations that was lost under the previous leader   Kerry said   I am pleased that Mr  Zoellick seems to enjoy the support of the international community and brings a diverse range of skills and international experience to the position  He must dedicate himself to healing the divisions caused by his predecessor and move the World Bank forward in its primary mission of helping those most in need around the world                                                                                       20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
