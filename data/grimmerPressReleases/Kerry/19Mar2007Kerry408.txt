       Releases                                                                                                                                           03 19 2007 Newton  Boston College Law School                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Senator Kerry commemorated the 75th Anniversary of his alma mater  Boston College Law School  delivering the keynote address at the last in a series of commemorative events  After making remarks  the Senator answered questions from the audience of more than 300 students  faculty and administrators  The event  titled Achieving Bipartisanship  The Challenge for National Leadership  also included a panel discussion featuring current and former members of Congress  all BC Law alumni  former Senator Warren Rudman of New Hampshire  Massachusetts Congressmen Stephen Lynch  Edward Markey  and Michael Capuano  and U S  Representatives Paul Hodes of New Hampshire and Robert Bobby Scott of Virginia  Senator Kerry then attended a meeting on Alternative Energy with labor leaders hosted by IBEW Local 103  There  participants discussed the impact of new and emerging technology on the Massachusetts workforce and talked about exciting new breakthroughs in alternative energy                                                                          20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
