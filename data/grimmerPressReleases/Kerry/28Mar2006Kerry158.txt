       Releases                                                                                                                                           03 28 2006 Statement by John Kerry on Resignation of Andrew Card                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     From Holbrook to the White House  Andy Card has spent nearly three decades doing what he believed was right for Massachusetts and our country   At home in Massachusetts  the bipartisan effort he led with Phil Johnston to clean up corruption in the State House set a high standard  and more of that bipartisan spirit is sadly needed in Washington today   It   s testimony to Andy   s fierce loyalty to the Bush family that in 1988  he moved to New Hampshire and slept on a cot in a tiny room above the campaign headquarters   For those of us in politics who know how hard it is to be away from family to do the things we care about  we appreciate the sacrifices Andy made  and hope that after so many years at such a breakneck speed he   ll be able to relax with Kathleene and their children and grandchildren                                                                                      20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
