       Releases                                                                                                                                           08 13 2007 Kerry on Walpole Team heading to Little League World Series                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            BOSTON   Senator John Kerry today issued the following statement on the Walpole Little League Team advancing to the Little League World Series        With the incredible performance of these young players  it s safe to say that now Morgan Magic isn t the only great baseball story to come out of Walpole        What a time to be from Massachusetts  Our state is home to the first place Red Sox  the three time world champion Patriots  the new Celtic trio of Kevin Garnett  Ray Allen and Paul Pierce   and now the Walpole American 12 year old All Stars         These Walpole ballplayers are remarkable   The countless hours of practice  teamwork and camaraderie both on and off the field speaks volumes about their character  In an age of tainted home run records in the major leagues  these kids went out and reminded us what baseball should be about         Although as a Sox fan I m a little worried that three of the players say Derek Jeter is their favorite slugger  there s no doubt these young athletes have already made Massachusetts proud  We wish them the best of luck in the World Series                                                                           20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
