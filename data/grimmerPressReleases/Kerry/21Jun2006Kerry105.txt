       Releases                                                                                                                                           06 21 2006 JOHN KERRY  WASHINGTON OUT OF TOUCH WITH MILLIONS OF AMERICANS TRYING TO MAKE ENDS MEET                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Every American deserves a shot at the American dream   Washington shows just how out of touch it is when Congress gives itself a raise time and again but won   t do the same for millions of workers           Since President Bush took office  the number of Americans living in poverty has increased by more than 5 million  Today 37 million Americans live in poverty  13 million of them are children  most of whom live with at least one working parent   That   s not how things should be in America  No American working full time should live below the poverty line  No one should be forced to choose between paying for either food or rent  their children   s health care or electricity    But instead of providing people with realistic wages for hard working Americans  the Republican leadership in Washington has repeatedly turned its back on these families by refusing to raise the minimum wage in almost 10 years          No one has fought harder to raise the minimum wage than Ted Kennedy  and I applaud all his decades long commitment to making this a reality                                                                                      20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
