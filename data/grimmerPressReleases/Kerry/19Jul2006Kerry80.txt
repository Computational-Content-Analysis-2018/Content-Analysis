       Releases                                                                                                                                           07 19 2006 Senate Committee Passes Kerry Amendment on Big Dig Investigation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       BOSTON     Senator John Kerry today offered an amendment to the National Transportation Safety Board authorization bill that passed the Senate Commerce Committee      The amendment directs the Department of Transportation   s Office of the Inspector General to launch an independent investigation looking into the Big Dig   s contractors and the public agencies that oversaw their work to determine whether poor planning  construction  and oversight led to last week   s tragic accident          We need to ensure that those responsible for the design and construction of the collapsed tunnel will be held accountable     said Senator Kerry     An independent investigation is the best way to guarantee there are no politically fueled motives  It   s high time we get to the bottom of this         The amendment also calls on the DOT IG to oversee investigations by the state and the Federal Highway Administration as well as any future investigations  and requires the IG to conduct a comprehensive safety review of the entire project                                                                                    20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
