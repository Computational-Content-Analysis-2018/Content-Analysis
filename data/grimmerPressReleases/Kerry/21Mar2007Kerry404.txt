       Releases                                                                                                                                           03 21 2007 John Kerry on Senate President Robert Travaglini s Resignation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         BOSTON   Senator John Kerry today released the following statement on Senate President Robert Travaglini s resignation        For 23 years  Trav has led the fight to improve access to prescription drugs for the elderly  fought to keep faith with our veterans  stood up to raise the minimum wage  and has championed children s causes with incredible passion  As Senate president  he was committed to passing health care reform and didn t stop until a Republican Governor had to sign that bill  His work on stem cell research serves as a model law for states across the nation            Gritty  tough  compassionate  and incredibly determined  Trav will be remembered as one of the most effective leaders the State Senate has ever known  I wish him well as he moves on to this new  exciting chapter in his life  He will surely be missed on Beacon Hill                                                                           20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
