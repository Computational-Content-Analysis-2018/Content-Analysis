       Releases                                                                                                                                           06 09 2005 SENATOR KERRY CONGRATULATES OUTSTANDING NCO OF THE YEAR STAFF SERGEANT ADAM BERG                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       WASHINGTON  DC  Today  Senator Kerry met with the Outstanding NCO of the Year  Staff Sergeant Adam Berg of North Attleboro  Staff Sergeant Berg  an infantryman from HQ and HQ Company 1st Battalion 182d Infantry of the Massachusetts Army Guard  was one of only 15 members of the Army National Guard honored  Berg won the Non Commissioned Officer competition for Massachusetts and the First Army North  including units from New York to Maine  To earn this prestigious award Berg undertook comprehensive levels of competition  including weapons qualifications  physical fitness testing  and an appearance before a board of senior NCOs  Staff Sergeant Berg is in Washington  D C  this week to be honored at the National Guard Outstanding Soldiers and Airmen of the Year ceremonies         Photographs taken today of Senator Kerry and Staff Sergeant Berg can be found at a href  http   kerry senate gov v3 press downloads html  http   kerry senate gov v3 press downloads html                                                                                        20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
