       Releases                                                                                                                                           09 27 2007 Kerry  Massachusetts to receive more than  11 million in home heating aid                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              BOSTON  Senator John Kerry today announced that Massachusetts would receive  11 941 698 as part of the Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program  LIHEAP   The funding will help assist low income homeowners and renters meet energy related needs this fall and winter    This money is a lifeline for many Massachusetts families during cold hard winters  No one should have to worry where their next meal is coming from or risk going without medical care just so they can heat their homes  Unfortunately  without LIHEAP many senior citizens and low income families would have to make those painful choices every winter  These funds will help thousands of families keep the heat on this winter  said Senator Kerry    The funding is part of  131 million in emergency assistance being award throughout the country                                                                                20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
