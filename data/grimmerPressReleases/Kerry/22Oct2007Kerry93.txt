       Releases                                                                                                                                           10 22 2007 Kerry  Kennedy  Delahunt announce  2 6 million in federal grants for Cape Cod National Seashore                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Boston  Senators John Kerry and Edward Kennedy and Congressman William Delahunt today announced that the Cape Cod National Seashore will receive  2 650 000 from the U S  Department of Transportation s Federal Transit Administration to fund the Seashore s alternative transportation systems  The funding will allow the Cape Cod National Seashore to purchase buses and trolleys and complete the planning for a more integrated parking and transit system       The Cape Cod National Seashore is one of the commonwealth s ecological treasures  Senator Kerry said  This funding will allow the Park Service to create a more user friendly experience for visitors to the Seashore while also ensuring that the fragile ecosystem of the area is preserved  I commend the Department of Transportation for investing in one of America s natural masterpieces      The Seashore is an extraordinary jewel of the Commonwealth  said Senator Kennedy  We owe it to future generations to be good stewards of our national treasures and make them open and accessible to all who visit them  These funds will help modernize the transportation system and increase access to the region for generations to come     Congressman Delahunt said  I want to congratulate the Cape Cod National Seashore  the Regional Transit Authority and everyone working with the Public Transit Task Force for helping expand and improve public transportation in our region and especially on the Lower Cape  I have enjoyed working with this partnership and am very pleased to see that these joint efforts have now become a national model
