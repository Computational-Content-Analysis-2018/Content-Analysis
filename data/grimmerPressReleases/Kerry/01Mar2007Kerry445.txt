       Releases                                                                                                                                           03 01 2007 Statement by John Kerry on the Passing of Arthur Schlesinger                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Arthur Schlesinger was at once idealist  story teller  public servant  and that most extraordinary of scholars who both recorded history and was witness to it          Schlesinger made no apologies for his dedication to the great progressive causes of our time  His chronicles of The New Deal and The New Frontier reminded us what our government and our leaders can and should always aspire to be  From the war in Vietnam to the Cold War to the war in Iraq  this wise man s voice spoke truth to the most difficult and often divisive of issues         Arthur Schlesinger s legacy amounts to a body of work even larger than the books he left behind  He helped shape public policy  he helped challenge generations of activists and thinkers  and he was never afraid to ask the tough questions  no matter how unpopular          Teresa and I are thinking of his family at this time  especially his sons whom we have been privileged to know  Stephen and Rob  who have both carried on his work in their own special way                                                                                       20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
