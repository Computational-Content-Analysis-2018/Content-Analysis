       Releases                                                                                                                                           01 16 2006 Statement by Senator John Kerry in Honor of Dr  Martin Luther King  Jr                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Below is a statement from Senator Kerry in honor of Dr  King and Martin Luther King Day                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Martin Luther King  Jr  gave his life to give the world an amazing gift   It   s our job to use it   With crime rates increasing  genocide in Darfur continuing  and Washington growing increasingly apathetic to the problems of real Americans  there   s no more urgent time to remember and live by Dr  King   s legacy of love and respect             It   s our privilege and our job to keep his legacy alive and to live with the same sense of conscience and the same determination to change our America for the better   The best way to honor the memory of Dr  King is to finish his work at home and around the world   Realizing Dr  King   s dream means health care without disparity and true equal voting rights for all   It also means that every American has a responsibility to stand up to the injustice of discrimination and the indifference that leaves too many behind   Because injustice anywhere hurts all of us  everywhere         It   s time to make Dr  King   s America our America
