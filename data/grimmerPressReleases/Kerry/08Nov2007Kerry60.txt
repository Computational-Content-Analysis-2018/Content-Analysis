       Releases                                                                                                                                           11 08 2007 Kerry announces  1 8 million in grants for MIT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Funds will support research into  next generation  solar power                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Boston  Senator John Kerry today announced that Massachusetts Institute of Technology will receive two U S  Department of Energy grants totaling  1 799 998 to fund research into next generation solar technology  The school will receive  900 000 to fund a project seeking to boost the performance of conventional solar cells and  899 998 to support research on silicon wafer making technology that would set new standards of electronic quality and cost    Americans realize what s at stake if we don t work together and confront global climate change  Senator Kerry said  It is essential that our country breaks its addiction to fossil fuels and takes advantage of renewable  clean energy sources  This funding will allow researchers at MIT to create more efficient  more cost effective solar power technology and give more Americans access to renewable energy                                                                                20510
