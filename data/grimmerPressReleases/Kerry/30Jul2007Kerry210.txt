       Releases                                                                                                                                           07 30 2007 Kerry praises Massachusetts company on being named a Green Power Partner by EPA                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Boston  Senator John Kerry today congratulated Framingham based Staples  Inc  on being named one of the United States Environmental Protection Agency s Top 25 Green Power Partners  The EPA s Green Power Partnership works with corporations and organizations that voluntarily buy green power from renewable sources including solar  wind and geothermal  Staples  Inc  purchased 121 800 000 kilowatt hours per year of green power  the 17th highest total on the EPA s list    I am proud that Massachusetts is home to one of America s most environmentally responsible companies  Senator Kerry said  One of the biggest misconceptions about the ecological challenges facing America is the assumption that environmental progress and economic growth are incompatible  Nothing could be farther from the truth  Staples  Inc  has clearly shown that being good to the environment is good for the bottom line  I commend the management and employees of Staples  Inc  for their record of corporate responsibility and urge other businesses to follow their lead  said Senator Kerry                                                                                20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
