       Releases                                                                                                                                           03 11 2005 Vote Kids Lends Support to Kids First Bill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Hearings Critical to America   s Children and Families                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Washington  DC   Today Vote Kids is pleased to join with Senator Kerry  hundreds of thousands of citizen co sponsors  and other endorsing organizations  in requesting that Senate Finance Chair Charles Grassley of Iowa schedule committee hearings on S 114     the Kids First Act     Vote Kids joins with Senator Kerry as he issues a letter to Chairman Grassley that clearly demonstrates the widespread support for children   s healthcare legislation     legislation that will save children  help families  and bolster both local communities and the nation as a whole     Said Vote Kids President and Founder Michael Petit     From one end of the country to the other I have seen repeatedly the fear and worry of parents who don   t know how they can pay for their children   s health care   whether a child needs cancer treatment or simply medicine for an earache    these stories can be prevented if our elected officials decide to put children first    Senator Kerry has our full and enthusiastic support for his landmark bill   It is an investment in our children  it is an investment in our nation   s future  and it is the right thing for our country to undertake without delay        Vote Kids is a non partisan  non profit organization headquartered in Washington  DC devoted to making children a political priority in American politics and to providing voters with the information they need to hold elected officials accountable for their records on children and family issues  For more information please visit us online at www votekids org
