       Releases                                                                                                                                           03 25 2005 SENATOR KERRY ANNOUNCES  267 986 STUDENT SUPPORT SERVICES GRANT FOR MASSACHUSETTS COLLEGE OF LIBERAL ARTS                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Senator Kerry announced today that The Massachusetts College of Liberal Arts has received a  267 986 portion of the Student Support Services Grant   This Grant will fund the Individual Enrichment Program  IEP    For over twenty years the IEP at Massachusetts College of Liberal Arts has provided comprehensive services to disadvantaged and learning disabled students who are often the first in their families to attend college   The students take part in a special four week summer program designed to teach them the skills they will need to succeed in college   The program also provides for specially trained college staff members to remain in contact with the students through their entire four years of college        I congratulate the Mass College of Liberal Arts community on their continued efforts to make higher education accessible to all students   By welcoming and supporting first generation  disabled  and low income students in college  the promise of opportunity is made real   With this federal investment  and the hard work of faculty and staff  the lives of these students and their families are forever improved
