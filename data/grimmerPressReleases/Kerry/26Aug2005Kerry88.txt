NATIONAL GUARD BASE   Today Senator John Kerry participated in a press conference on the Base Realignment and Closure  BRAC  Commission   s decision to close Otis Air National Guard Base and replace the A 10 fighter planes at Barnes Air National Guard Base with 18 F 15 fighters        Below is Senator Kerry   s statement on the BRAC Commission   s decision on Otis and Barnes Air National Guard Bases        The Commission s decision makes no sense   I will continue to explore congressional and legal options to keep Otis open and keep the A 10s at Barnes   The Fighter Wings at Otis and Barnes are superb units that play a critical role in our defense and homeland security   I m also concerned that the Commission s decision will undermine the ability of the Coast Guard to continue their life
