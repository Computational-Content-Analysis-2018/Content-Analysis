       Releases                                                                                                                                           09 05 2007 Kerry on reopening of new Comeau Bridge in Haverhill                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   BOSTON  Senator John Kerry  today released the following statement on the reopening of the Sgt  Joseph  Comeau Bridge in Haverhill  I am proud to join with Mayor Fiorentini and the  residents of Haverhill in celebrating the  reopening of the Sergeant Joseph Comeau Bridge    For almost 30 years  the bridge has honored the memory  of a courageous veteran  and today  after a five year renovation  the bridge is  set to re open  The new bridge will not only alleviate traffic congestion but it  will continue to pay tribute to a local hero   When Sgt  Comeaus country needed him  he answered the  call and served heroically in the Pacific during World War II  When the fighting  was over  he returned home and immersed himself in the Haverhill community  The  bridge honors him  and all local veterans who served our country from December  1940 to December 1945  There is no greater honor than serving ones country and  Haverhills new  bridge will be a constant reminder of such  service                                                                               20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
