       Releases                                                                                                                                           03 17 2007 Boston and Northampton                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Senator Kerry attended a sendoff ceremony at the Westin Seaport Hotel for the 719th Movement Control Battalion  There  he had the opportunity to speak with more than 60 soldiers and their families to thank them for their selfless service to our country  The Unit departed Boston for Fort Bragg  where it will prepare for its eventual deployment to Iraq  Senator Kerry said  These men and women are our best and our bravest  They are leaving behind their families and friends to do their duty  They need to know that Massachusetts and their country appreciate their service  and that we will keep our promise to them when they return home  Senator Kerry then traveled to Northampton to tour a VA Medical Center and meet with patients and staff  He also visited the United Veterans of America homeless shelter at the VA Medical Center where he was thanked for donating a 53inch television just in time for the Super Bowl                                                                          20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
