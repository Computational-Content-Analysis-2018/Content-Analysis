       Releases                                                                                                                                           12 13 2005 John Kerry Honors Latino Chamber of Commerce Business Center                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           WASHINGTON   Senator John Kerry entered the following statement into the Congressional Record yesterday commending the opening of the Latino Chamber of Commerce   s new Business Center in Springfield         Mr  President   I wish to congratulate the Massachusetts Latino Chamber of Commerce  La C  mara de Comercio  on the opening of their new Business Center in Springfield  Massachusetts   The Business Center will be the home of a number of Latino owned businesses as well as provide owners with workshops and training for members to gain knowledge in their perspective business area   In addition  the Center will establish a computer lab to assist members with business research and planning  The new Center will serve as resource able to provide support  referral  and advocacy services to small business owners   La C  mara de Comercio will establish a computer center to assist members with business research and planning  as well as business language seminars   Latino businesses across the Commonwealth from Springfield to Boston are helping us to better compete in the global market   It is my privilege to congratulate La C  mara de Comercio on this new endeavor
