       Releases                                                                                                                                           04 24 2007 Kerry Urges Equal Wages for Women on Equal Pay Day                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON D C    Senator John Kerry made the following statement today  Equal Pay Day   The day highlights the fact that women continue to only make 77 cents for every dollar a man makes  with women of color on average earning even less   Equal Pay Day  April 24  2007  symbolizes how many extra days into the year a woman would have to work to earn as much as a man earned in 2006          Every American deserves a fair shot at the American dream  and women should not have to work harder and longer than men to get the pay they deserve   Senator Kerry said   Hard working women in our country  and anyone who has a daughter  a wife  or a mother who contributes to the family income  knows how vital this fight is today  and for our tomorrow  Equality is an American value we must never turn our backs on                                                                                       20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
