       Releases                                                                                                                                           05 05 2005 Statement by Senator John Kerry In  Observance of Holocaust Remembrance Day                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Holocaust Remembrance Day is a time to honor and remember the millions of innocent lives lost in one of the darkest periods in all of world history  It should also be a reaffirmation of our vow to never forget the evil that comes from bigotry and intolerance  We have a responsibility to stand up and affirm human dignity everywhere around the globe where it is in peril  We can never forget or ignore what happened during the Holocaust  and we must never allow it to be repeated    any time or anywhere                                                                                        20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
