       Releases                                                                                                                                           05 09 2007 Kerry Votes for FDA Bill to Make Medications Safer for Americans                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Bill Also Gives FDA Power to Order Recalls on Contaminated Pet Food                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               WASHINGTON D C    Sen  John Kerry said today that new legislation to overhaul the Food and Drug Administration will help ensure that medications sold in this country are safe and properly marketed  Kerry voted for the bill this afternoon and said he hopes that Congressional leaders can get a final version of the legislation to the White House quickly for final approval        The FDA overhaul bill includes a number of other important provisions  such as requiring new studies before drugs are sold to the public  it gives the FDA power to require label changes on medications and  allows the agency to restrict how the drug is sold  Kerry said the bill should also address some of the concern from pet owners by giving the FDA authority to order immediate recalls and also to create strict new standards for reporting incidents of contamination         This bill provides vital financing for the FDA at a time when we desperately need to improve the safety of our food and prescription drugs   Kerry said   It also allows us to continue with the safe development and dissemination of pharmaceuticals for children  while adding new protections for our nation s food supply  I commend Sen  Kennedy for his work on this important legislation and I look forward to getting a final version of this bill to the President as quickly as possible
