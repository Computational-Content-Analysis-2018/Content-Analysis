       Releases                                                                                                                                           10 18 2005 STATEMENT FROM SENATOR JOHN KERRY ON TAUNTON EVACUATION                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Senator John Kerry released the following statement after speaking with Mayor Bob Nunes of Taunton last night                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Mayor Nunes took the vital steps in declaring a state of emergency and evacuating the area near the Whittendon Pond Dam yesterday in an effort to prevent a major catastrophe in Taunton  As the structural integrity of the Whittendon Pond Dam is in question  the federal government should be prepared to provide whatever resources and assistance necessary to protect the people and property of Taunton  With the devastating floods in Massachusetts this past week and the developing situation in Taunton we must have the ability to act quickly to assist our communities in need                                                                                      20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
