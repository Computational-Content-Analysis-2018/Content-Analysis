       Releases                                                                                                                                           06 30 2006 Boston                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Joined by fellow prostate cancer survivor and founder of the Prostate Health Education Network  Tom Farrington  and his daughter  Vanessa Kerry  Senator Kerry outlined his plan for providing health care to all Americans in a speech delivered in historic Faneuil Hall  The Senator stated his belief that the nation has an obligation to make health care a right for all Americans regardless of income  and he outlined a plan to tackle the three major challenges in health care spiraling costs  shrinking coverage  and uneven quality  The plan would mandate health coverage for all U S  children  control costs through investments in moneysavings technologies  reinsurance  and publicprivate partnerships  and invest in preventative care to improve health outcomes and reduce costs                                                                          20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
