       Releases                                                                                                                                           02 09 2005 John Kerry on Medicare Costs Estimates and the Health Care Crisis in America                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Below is a statement from Senator John Kerry today on news that that costs for the Medicare prescription drug plan will be more than twice what the White House originally estimated          News that taxpayers will be billed for more than twice what the White House estimated for their Medicare prescription drug plan is just the latest evidence that the real crisis facing America is health care   Eleven million children are uninsured and record numbers of families are declaring bankruptcy because of their health care costs  With soaring costs and declining access to care  we have reached a dangerous tipping point and America s leaders must act        Instead of addressing this real growing health care crisis  across the country  the White House wants to change the subject to the crisis they are trying to create around Social Security   If the numbers don t add up for Medicare  how can we be sure they will add up for Social Security  Our job as leaders is to work together to ensure the economic security of all Americans  not put their lives and health more at risk                                                                                    20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
