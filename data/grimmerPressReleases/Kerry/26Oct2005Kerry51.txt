       Releases                                                                                                                                           10 26 2005 Statement from John Kerry on Senate Defeat of LIHEAP Amendment                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Senator John Kerry released the following statement today Senate defeat of LIHEAP Amendment                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         As the days get shorter and temperatures continue to drop  it s unbelievable that the Senate continues to stand in the way of common sense solutions to the home heating crisis coming upon us this winter  American families and seniors are going to be forced to choose between their very basic needs this winter   the choice between putting food on the table and paying heating bills is real  and will affect too many families this winter        It s long overdue for Republican leaders in Washington get their priorities straight   before it s too late  When schools can t afford to heat their classrooms  seniors scrimp on medical costs to heat their homes and parents worry how to make ends meet to keep their families warm and fed  it s clear we ve truly failed our families  Republicans in Congress need to act now  put the politics aside that got us into this heating crisis  and finally do what s right for our kids and seniors
