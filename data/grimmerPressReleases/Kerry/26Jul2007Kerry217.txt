       Releases                                                                                                                                           07 26 2007 Kerry supports family of Iraq war veteran in wrongful death lawsuit                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Lucey family of Belchertown  Massachusetts sues VA s Nicholson                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      BOSTON  Senator  John Kerry today released the following statement on the wrongful death lawsuit  filed by Kevin and Joyce Lucey against Veterans Affairs Secretary  Jim  Nicholson  The Luceys son Jeff committed suicide in June of 2004 after  returning from Iraq   The Luceys have worked tirelessly to prevent this from  happening to even one more family  and I will always support them in their  efforts to prevent this horrible tragedy from occurring again  I hope their  lawsuit is a wake up call to both the Bush Administration and the VA they need  to get their priorities straight  Far too many of our brave soldiers are coming  home and not getting the help they need  Our current policy has failed far too  many families and brave men and women in uniform  and I applaud the Luceys for  their unceasing effort to prevent what happened to their son from happening to  another American family and another soldier in need of  help   Since Jeffs death  Kerry has been working with the  Lucey family in helping to highlight the inadequacies of our nations treatment  of returning veterans                                                                                20510
