       Releases                                                                                                                                           08 17 2005 SENATORS KERRY AND KENNEDY ANNOUNCE FEDERAL GRANT TO WEST SPRINGFIELD POLICE DEPARTMENT FOR FINGERPRINTING TECHNOLOGY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Senators John Kerry and Edward Kennedy today announced a federal grant of  16 158 for West Springfield s Police Department to purchase new fingerprinting technology        Senator Kerry said   The men and women of the West Springfield Police Department work hard every day to serve and protect our community  They deserve the most up to date technology to help them fight crime  and I m happy we could help them get it        Senator Kennedy said   The police officers of West Springfield put their lives on the line each day to prevent and combat crime  The Department deserves the best in equipment and technology  so that it can continue its outstanding work          This grant is made possible under the U S  Justice Department s Edward Byrne Memorial Justice Grant Assistance Program                                                                           20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
