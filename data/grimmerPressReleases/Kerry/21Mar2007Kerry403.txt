       Releases                                                                                                                                           03 21 2007 John Kerry on Therese Murray s election as President of the Massachusetts Senate                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       BOSTON   Senator John Kerry today released the following statement on Senator Therese Murray s election as President of the Massachusetts Senate        Another barrier has broken in Massachusetts  The first woman to lead either chamber of the legislature  Terry Murray is going to make an enormous impact and set an important example  As a woman and a voice from the South Shore  Terry will bring a fresh perspective to the State Senate presidency         She has long been an advocate for human service programs  has fought for increased funding for our state s schools  accessible and affordable health care  and improved public safety  I look forward to working with her and wish her the very best of luck in her new position                                                                           20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
