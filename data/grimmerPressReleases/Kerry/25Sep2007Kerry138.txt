       Releases                                                                                                                                           09 25 2007 Kerry on Quincy s bold plan to end homelessness                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        BOSTON  Senator John Kerry today issued the following statement on the permanent closing of a womens shelter due to success of Quincy 10 Year Plan to End Homelessness    After two years of hard work  Quincys 10 year plan to end homelessness is making great progress  The closing of the Emergency Shelter for homeless women in Quincy proves that with creative and determined policy innovations  homelessness can be addressed  and not just accepted as a tragic way of life  The big new idea of moving away from merely sheltering and instead focusing on helping people find permanent  supportive housing is a model for cities throughout Massachusetts and can be a national model too   Todays announcement is a tribute to Mayor Phelan  Father Bills Place and the entire Quincy community                                                                          20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
