       Releases                                                                                                                                           09 17 2007 Kerry  Kennedy  and McGovern announce  1 900 000 grant for Attleboro Redevelopment Authority                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           BOSTON  Senators John Kerry and Edward Kennedy today  along with Congressman James McGovern  announced that the Attleboro Redevelopment Authority will receive  1 943 557 from the U S  Department of Transportation s Federal Transit Administration  FTA  for the construction of the Attleboro Intermodal Transportation Center  The center will be located next to the Attleboro commuter rail station and will house a new bus terminal for the Greater Attleboro Taunton Regional Transit Authority s local bus and van services  The grant also funds the construction of a 900 space structured parking garage and 180 space surface lot to accommodate commuter rail patrons  This most recent grant brings the FTA s commitment for this project to  7 396 439      The tremendous environmental and economic benefits of public transportation can only be realized if the public actually has easy access to public transportation options  Senator Kerry said  This funding will make public transportation a more realistic option for many people in the Attleboro area and I commend the DOT for investing in our states public transportation system      Smart transportation investments are vital to Attleboros quality of life and its economy  Senator Kennedy said  This needed federal funding is an opportunity to make lasting transportation improvements      Im very pleased that Attleboro is continuing to move forward with this important project  Rep  McGovern said  The Intermodal Transportation Center will serve as a catalyst in re energizing a vibrant downtown Attleboro
