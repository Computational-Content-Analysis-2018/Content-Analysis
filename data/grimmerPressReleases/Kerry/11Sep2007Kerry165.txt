       Releases                                                                                                                                           09 11 2007 Kerry Statement on 6th Anniversary of 9 11                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            WASHINGTON  D C   Sen  John Kerry issued the following statement to mark Patriot Day  the day when the nation commemorates the losses of Sept  11  2001    As we pause today to remember the loss of nearly 3 000 American lives six years ago  we honor everyone we lost to the worst terrorist attack ever on American soil  Kerry said  Its deeply frustrating that even on this day we are reminded that the murderer behind those attacks  Osama bin Laden  is still free to spread propaganda while al Qaeda continues to plot  plan  and proselytize  This should be a reminder that while it is right to remember those we loved and lost on 9 11  it is also past time for the government to recommit itself to bringing their killers to justice                                                                          20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
