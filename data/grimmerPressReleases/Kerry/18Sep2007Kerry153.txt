       Releases                                                                                                                                           09 18 2007 Kerry Statement on Florida Campus Incident                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             WASHINGTON D C   Sen  John Kerry issued the following statement today  in response to the arrest of a student at the University of Florida     In 37 years of public appearances  through wars  protests and highly emotional events  I have never had a dialogue end this way  I believe I could have handled the situation without interruption  but I do not know what warnings or other exchanges transpired between the young man and the police prior to his barging to the front of the line and their intervention  I asked the police to allow me to answer the question and was in the process of responding when he was taken into custody  I was not aware that a taser was used until after I left the building  I hope that neither the student nor any of the police were injured  I regret enormously that a good healthy discussion was interrupted                                                                          20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
