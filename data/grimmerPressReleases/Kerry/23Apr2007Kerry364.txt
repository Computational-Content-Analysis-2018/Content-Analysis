       Releases                                                                                                                                           04 23 2007 Kerry Bill will make Capitol Complex Carbon Neutral by 2020                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Kerry Leads Charge to Make Congress Walk the Walk on Pollution  Clean Energy                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        WASHINGTON  DC   Today  Senator John Kerry  D Mass   announced that he will introduce a bill to set a goal of making the Capitol Complex   the Senate  House of Representatives  U S  Capitol and surrounding buildings in Washington  DC   carbon neutral by 2020          A Saturday article in The Washington Post reported that the Capitol Power Plant  a main energy source for the Capitol  Senate  House and other buildings in the Capitol Complex  is the city s second largest source of carbon monoxide  sulfur dioxide  and other dirty compounds that contribute to the high rates of pollution in the district  Washington  DC has repeatedly been in violation of the Clean Air Act and suffers some of the highest rates of asthma in the country          It s time the Congress starts to walk the walk when it comes to fighting climate change and saving energy   Senator Kerry said    The very plant that fuels our offices and the Capitol is contributing to high levels of pollution and affecting families who live in the city   We need to lead by example on the environment by setting a bold goal of making our Capitol and Congress energy efficient and fighting for clean coal and renewable sources of energy         Kerry s bill will establish a goal that by 2020 the Capitol Complex will be carbon neutral  This goal will be achieved through a combination of energy efficiency gains  stepping up conservation measures  and using both onsite and offsite renewable sources of energy          In the shadow of the nation s capital  we should expect more than a dirty power plant that pollutes the air and our communities   Senator Kerry said    Taking action on the Capitol Power Plant and changing our energy use at the Capitol buildings will not only fight the high rates of asthma and poor air quality in the city  but also combat global climate change   This is action that s long overdue  and I will fight to make our Capitol a model for the rest of the country
