       Releases                                                                                                                                           09 25 2007 Kerry on release of  Nation s Report Card  grading Massachusetts students                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              BOSTON   Senator John Kerry released the following statement on the 2007 National Assessment of Education Progress results  which were announced by Governor Patrick earlier today      Massachusetts students continue to rank among the best in the nation  which is a tribute to our teachers  parents  administrators and our students themselves       We should take pride in these latest results  but we must also realize that there is still a lot of work to be done  MCAS results released earlier this month found evidence of widespread disparities  Too many minority students and those with limited English proficiency have been left outside these recent gains  We cannot have an education system that is separate and unequal       We must also make certain that our schools have everything they need from manageable class sizes  modern technology  and ongoing professional development for our teachers  These critical resources will keep education in Massachusetts among the best of the best       I thank Governor Patrick for his leadership in continuing to put our children first and for working to close the achievement gap  His commitment to a good education starting at an early age is a model every single child in our state will benefit from                                                                                  20510
