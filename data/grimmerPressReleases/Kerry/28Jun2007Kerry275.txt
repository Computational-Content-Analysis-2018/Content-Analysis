       Releases                                                                                                                                           06 28 2007 Kerry Congratulates North Andover Company for Being Awarded as One of the Top Small Businesses in America                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              BOSTON  Senator John Kerry  Chairman of the Senate Committee on Small Business and Entrepreneurship today announced that Moody  Famiglietti  Andronico  LLP of North Andover has been awarded by the Society for Human Resource Management  SHRM  and the Great Places to Work Institute as part of the annual list of the 50 Best Small  Medium Companies to Work for in America     Moody  Famiglietti   Andronico  LLP is a leader in providing a positive work environment to their employees  said Senator Kerry  I am thrilled that it has been recognized for its hard work and I wish it continued success in improving its entrepreneurial endeavors throughout Massachusetts      Moody  Famiglietti  Andronico  LLP is an accounting firm that says its prides itself on its positive environment and putting people first       This is the fourth year that SHRM has released their list of 50 Best Small  Medium Companies to Work for in America  The results were based off of employee surveys and other questionnaires that detail the environment of these various companies                                                                                 20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
