       Releases                                                                                                                                           07 12 2005 SENATOR JOHN KERRY AND SENATOR EDWARD KENNEDY ANNOUNCE  13 709 GRANT TO BOLSTER LAW ENFORCEMENT EFFORTS IN WARREN                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      The Edward Byrne Memorial Justice Assistance Grant  JAG  program aims to prevent and control crime  JAG funds can be used to aid programs that concentrate on law enforcement  prosecution and courts  prevention and education  corrections and community corrections  drug treatment  and technology improvement  The Warren Police Department will utilize the allocations from the Bureau of Justice Assistance this year to concentrate on pivotal measures such as the effort toward mitigation of domestic violence and the need to make technology more adaptable to drug related crime analysis       Senator Kerry said     I am proud to announce the Warren Police Department as the recipient of the Edward Byrne Memorial Justice Assistance Grant  JAG   This grant will be used for domestic violence prevention training and for new technology that will more effectively aid law enforcement officials in Warren           Senator Kennedy said   I commend the Warren Police Department for its leadership in combating domestic violence  These funds will enable its officers to strengthen this important effort through additional training and the use of up to date computer software             30
