       Releases                                                                                                                                           06 21 2007 Kerry Applauds Rumored Closure of Guantanamo Bay Prison                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                WASHINGTON D C   Sen  John Kerry issued the following statement today  following reports that the White House and Pentagon were moving towards closing the Guantanamo Bay detainee facility  The President has steadfastly resisted calls from Sen  Kerry and others to close the much criticized detention camp  where roughly 380 alleged terrorists are now detained      If these reports are proved true  this is a long overdue step in removing the stain Guantanamo has left on our foreign policy  Kerry said  It also goes a long way toward returning America s moral authority in the world  and addressing the mistakes which have set us back in the fight against terrorism      The Guantanamo Bay prison was built in 2002 to hold terror suspects captured in military operations                                                                                20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
