       Releases                                                                                                                                           08 02 2007 Kerry urges passage of National Commission on Infrastructure legislation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Boston  Senator John Kerry today announced he is co sponsoring legislation introduced by Senator Tom Carper to create a National Commission on Infrastructure  The Commission would develop a long term plan to improve the state of Americas infrastructure and ensure that it meets rigorous safety standards    Wednesdays tragedy in Minneapolis is a sobering reminder of the lack of investment and attention given to our nations bridges and roads  Senator Kerry said  In this country  bridges shouldnt simply collapse  The American Society of Civil Engineers gave bridges a  C  in its 2005 Report Card due to the lack of funding available to repair them  This is unacceptable  Today I have co sponsored legislation that creates a National Commission on Infrastructure which will issue recommendations to help develop a plan to repair and maintain the nations roads  bridges  and dams  In light of the heartbreaking bridge collapse in Minneapolis  Congress should quickly pass this legislation and help ensure that a tragedy of this scale never happens again and I intend to work hard with my colleagues to bring this issue to the forefront as quickly as possible
