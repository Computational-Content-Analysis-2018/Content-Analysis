       Releases                                                                                                                                           06 28 2007 Kerry Congratulates Waltham Company for being named one of  50 Best Small   Medium Companies to Work for in America                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    BOSTON  Senator John Kerry Chairman of the Senate Committee on Small Business and Entrepreneurship today announced that the Massachusetts Medical Society of Waltham has been named one of the top small and medium companies to work for in America by the Society for Human Resource Management  SHRM  and the Great Places to Work Institute     The Massachusetts Medical Society has been a leader when it comes to focusing on the well being of its 410 employees  said Senator Kerry  I am thrilled it has been recognized for its policies of extended paid leave  onsite child care  and outstanding employee health coverage    The Massachusetts Medical Society is a non profit educational institute founded in 1781  Representatives of the company are available to discuss receiving this honor and how they made the Society one of the best companies to work for in America     It was selected from companies of 50 to 999 employees who use smart management strategies to maximize productivity while maintaining satisfaction among their workforce     The SHRM s list  which is in its fourth year  identifies 25 small and 25 medium sized companies that use successful management strategies and have highly productive  satisfied staffs  Each company was evaluated via employee responses to questions about motivation and workplace culture  The Massachusetts Medical Society was one of only three Massachusetts businesses granted a place on the list                                                                                  20510
