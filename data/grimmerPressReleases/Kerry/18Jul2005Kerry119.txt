       Releases                                                                                                                                           07 18 2005 Senator John Kerry Announces  8 037 000 Grant Awarded to the Massachusetts Victim Witness Assistance Board                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Today Senator John Kerry announced an  8 037 000 grant to the Massachusetts Victim Witness Assistance Board  This grant comes from the Crime Victims Fund that was established to enhance services to crime victims in each state  The grant is awarded to the state and distributed to local organizations that provide direct services to crime victims  These funds will be used for assistance services for victims and survivors of domestic violence  sexual assault  child abuse  drunk driving  homicide  and other crimes       Senator Kerry said     This grant will make a lasting impact on the Massachusetts criminal justice system by making it possible for state and local agencies to provide more comprehensive assistance and services to victims of violent crime   Because of this grant  services including  crisis intervention  counseling  emergency shelter  criminal justice advocacy and emergency transportation will be more readily available to victims  I applaud the Massachusetts Victim Witness Assistance Board for all they have accomplished and congratulate them on receiving this important grant                                                                                20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
