       Releases                                                                                                                                           09 25 2007 Kerry on Effort to Waive Cost of College Tuition and Fees for Massachusetts Veterans                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   BOSTON  Senator John Kerry today released the following statement on two bills being filed in the Massachusetts State Legislature      Massachusetts is setting an example that Washington should follow when it comes to keeping faith with our veterans  said Senator Kerry  It would be an insult to our states thousands of veterans if we allow them to struggle to afford a college education  A bill to waive the cost of tuition and fees for veterans who attend our states public colleges and universities is a commonsense way to ease the financial burden  This is one small price we can pay at a time when we ask so much of our brave men and women in uniform  I strongly urge the entire state legislature to support these bills                                                                                  20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
