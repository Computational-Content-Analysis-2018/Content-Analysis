       Releases                                                                                                                                           07 27 2007 Kerry Says 9 11 Reform Bill Improves Communication During Disaster                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON D C   Sen  John Kerry announced today that the 9 11 reform bill approved by the Congress includes two proposals he drafted       Kerrys provisions include efforts to integrate hospitals into our emergency communications network and to make preparations for the deployment of an emergency backup communications system to be used during a terrorist attack or natural disaster       The tragedies of 9 11 and Katrina taught us many life changing lessons  one being the urgent need to significantly improve our communications capabilities in a disaster  Senator Kerry said  When the communications system failed on 9 11  more lives were lost and unnecessary challenges were put in the way of our first responders  During Hurricane Katrina  Gulf Coast hospitals lost the ability to communicate  this bill will provide them with grant money to arm them with improved equipment                                                                                     20510                                                                                                                                                                                          Contact           Sitemap
