                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    STATEMENT BY U S  SENATOR BENJAMIN L  CARDIN ON HIS VOTE FOR THE IRAQ SUPPLEMENTAL SPENDING BILL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Friday  May 25  2007       I voted for the Supplemental Appropriations bill because  in the end  I am committed to fund our troops in harms way   But I am greatly disappointed      At each turn I have opposed the President s Iraq policies  from the initial vote to authorize action in that nation  to the most recent votes in the Senate calling for the near term redeployment of our troops   My commitment remains to change our mission in Iraq and bring our troops home
