                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    ON NOMINATION OF MICHAEL B  MUKASEY AS ATTORNEY GENERAL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Monday  September 17  2007      I am pleased that President Bush has moved quickly to nominate a new Attorney General  As a member of the Senate Judiciary Committee  I am looking forward to the confirmation process and to the opportunity to question Judge Mukasey  I want to ensure that he will provide the leadership that is necessary to return the professionalism and independence to the Department of Justice
