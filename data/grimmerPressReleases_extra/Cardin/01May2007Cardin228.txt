                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    STATEMENT BY U S  SENATOR BENJAMIN L  CARDIN  ON PRESIDENT S VETO OF THE IRAQ SUPPLEMENTAL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Tuesday  May 1  2007      Susan Sullam  410 960 2440     I am disappointed by the President s decision to veto the Iraq Supplemental bill   It provided  4 billion more for our soldiers than the President had requested  and it also contained a sensible strategy for getting U S  troops out of a civil war that has engulfed Iraq       The language included in the Iraq Supplemental spending bill was necessary because the President has failed to deal effectively with the growing violence and civil war that has consumed Iraq   I urge the President to work with Congress to find a way to bring our troops home as safely and quickly as possible   The American people do not want U S  troops in the midst of a civil war
