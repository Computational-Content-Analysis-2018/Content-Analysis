                                                                           CARDIN  MIKULSKI INTRODUCE RESOLUTION HONORING HOPKINS MEN S LACROSSE TEAM ON NCAA CHAMPIONSHIP VICTORY                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Wednesday  June 6  2007       Jesse Haladay  202 224 4524      WASHINGTON  U S  Senators Benjamin L  Cardin and Barbara A  Mikulski today introduced a resolution congratulating the Johns Hopkins University Blue Jays on winning the 2007 NCAA Division I Mens Lacrosse Championships    I am incredibly proud of the Johns Hopkins Mens Lacrosse team  said Senator Cardin  They played a close  intense game and fought until the very last second to secure their victory  From its origins as a Native American sport to professional lacrosse today  lacrosse is an integral part of Marylands culture and history  I join with my fellow Marylanders in congratulating the Blue Jays on their impressive victory    I am so proud of the young men of this lacrosse team  who showed Duke and the country why the Black and Blue now hold nine national championships  The Blue Jays  great victory shows the hard work  spirit and heart of an amazing team and the support of an outstanding University  said Senator Mikulski  Congratulations to every member of the men s lacrosse team  I thank the players and coaches for the excitement of such a wonderful season and I am looking forward to next year when you fight for a tenth championship title   This is the Blue Jays second NCAA Mens Lacrosse Championship victory in three years and ninth victory since 1974
