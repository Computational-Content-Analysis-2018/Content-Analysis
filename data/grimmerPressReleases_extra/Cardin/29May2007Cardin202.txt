                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    STATEMENT BY U S  SENATOR BENJAMIN L  CARDIN ON THE DEATH OF PARREN MITCHELL                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Tuesday  May 29  2007      Parren Mitchell was a giant of a man whom I greatly admired  He was fearless  determined and committed to breaking down racial barriers  His life was dedicated to fighting for justice and equal opportunity for all Americans  He was an inspiration to those of us who care about providing a voice to Americans who are often denied a chance at the American Dream
