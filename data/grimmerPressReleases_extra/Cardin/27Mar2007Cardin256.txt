                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    SEN  CARDIN NAMES SOUTHERN MARYLAND FIELD REPRESENTATIVE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Tuesday  March 27  2007      Susan Sullam  410 962 4436  Oren Shur  202 224 4524   WASHINGTON   U S  Sen  Benjamin L  Cardin today announced that he has named Hammad S  Matin as his Southern Maryland Field Representative   Starting April 1  Mr  Matin will represent the Senator in Charles  St  Mary s and Calvert counties  work with constituents on federal issues  coordinate with local and state officials  and handle community outreach     Mr Matin served as Assistant State s Attorney for Charles County for six years   He is currently chair of the Charles Co  Housing Commission  He is an active member of the 4th and 5th Democratic Club  New Western Democratic Club  Northern Democratic Club and the Charles County Young Democrats   He graduated cum laude from the University of Maryland  College Park  and is a graduate of the Washington   Lee University School of Law      Hammad will be my eyes and ears in Southern Maryland   He is experienced  dedicated and knowledgeable  and he has a keen insight into issues that are important to residents of Southern Maryland   said Sen  Cardin in announcing his appointment     The Senator has said that  Southern Maryland is facing a number of important challenges  particularly in the areas of development and growth  affordable housing and needed improvements to the transportation infrastructure  I plan to be very involved in making sure that Southern Maryland has the resources to address many of the these issues      Mr  Matin will work out of Sen  Cardin s Field Office in Bowie  Maryland
