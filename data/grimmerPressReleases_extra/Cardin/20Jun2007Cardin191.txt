                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    SENATOR CARDIN NAMES WESTERN MARYLAND FIELD REPRESENTATIVE                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Wednesday  June 20  2007      Susan Sullam  410 962 4436 or 410 960 2440  cell    WASHINGTON  U S  Senator Benjamin L  Cardin today announced that he has named Robin Summerfield as his Western Maryland Representative  Starting on July 9  Mr  Summerfield will represent the Senator in Garrett  Allegany  Washington and Frederick counties  work with constituents on federal issues  coordinate with local and state officials  and handle community outreach   Mr  Summerfield  a resident of Allegany County and a graduate of Frostburg State University  has extensive experience in the field of child welfare and juvenile services  He has served as a Systems of Care Specialist for The Local Management Board of Allegany County and is a former Regional Coordinator for the Alliance for Children  Inc  He also is President elect of the Maryland Chapter of National Association of Social Workers  He also has had small business experience working for family owned business Summerfield Aviation  which is located at Cumberland Regional Airport   Robin will be my eyes and ears in Western Maryland  He is experienced  dedicated and knowledgeable  He cares greatly and has a keen insight into issues that are important to residents of Western Maryland  said Senator Cardin in announcing his appointment   The Senator has said that the Western Maryland faces several important challenges  particularly in the areas of agriculture  transportation  economic development and jobs  I plan to be very involved in making sure that Western Maryland has the resources to address many of these issues
