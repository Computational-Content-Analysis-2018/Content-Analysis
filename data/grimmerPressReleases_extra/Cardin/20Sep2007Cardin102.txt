                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    CARDIN  MIKULSKI ANNOUNCE FUNDING FOR GARRETT COUNTY TRADE SHOW ENTERPRISE CENTER                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Thursday  September 20  2007       WASHINGTON  D C   Senators Benjamin L  Cardin and Barbara A  Mikulski  both D Md   today announced the Appalachian Regional Commission  ARC  has awarded  210 000 to the Garrett County Agriculture Fair Inc  for the Garrett County Trade Show Enterprise Center  The facility will support 20 new jobs and 80 existing businesses  not just with words  but with deeds   This funding will bring new jobs and new visitors to Garrett County  It will provide entertainment and economic development for the area for generations to come  said Senator Mikulski  I am proud to fight for an investment in Western Maryland communities  I am pleased that this ARC grant will provide additional funding for this important economic development project in Garrett County  said Senator Cardin  This new facility will allow Garrett County to host larger exhibits and meetings  which will help businesses in the region  The Garrett County Trade Show Enterprise Center will be a 30 000 square foot multi purpose facility that will be able to accommodate 800 people  It will host a wide range of events  including trade shows  exhibits  educational and tourist activities  More than 80 local businesses will also display and sell local products in the center  In addition to ARC funds  local sources will provide  365 000  The ARC is a federal state partnership that works with the people of Appalachia to create opportunities for self sustaining economic development and improved quality of life
