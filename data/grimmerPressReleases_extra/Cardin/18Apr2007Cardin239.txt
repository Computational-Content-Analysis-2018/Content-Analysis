                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    CARDIN AND MIKULSKI ANNOUNCE  2 2 MILLION FOR FREDERICK AIRPORT EXTENSION PROJECT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Long Term Project Will Accommodate Larger Aircraft    Wednesday  April 18  2007      Susan Sullam  410 962 4436   WASHINGTON   U S  Senators Benjamin L  Cardin and Barbara A  Mikulski announced today that the U S  Department of Transportation has awarded the Frederick Municipal Airport  2 2 million to purchase land for a runway extension   The extension would increase the runway from 5 220 feet to 6 000 feet         The Frederick Municipal Airport has become an important facility to the Washington region and Western Maryland   said Senator Cardin    This project is a win win for economic growth and development because it will enable larger aircraft with greater range and on board payload to utilize the airport       Our regional airports play a key role in maintaining Maryland s robust economy   This federal funding is an investment in Frederick County that will provide new opportunities today and tomorrow   said Senator Mikulski   I will keep fighting for the resources our Maryland communities need to foster growth and economic development      Construction on the runway extension project is expected to be a multi year project and will begin once the necessary land acquisition is completed
